## **Subscribe to a calendar**



<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/85-1.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

1. **<u>Open the context menu</u>**: In the calendar view, click on the three dots located at the top right corner.

2. **<u>Select subscribe</u>**: Under the context menu, select the “**Subscribe**” option.

   - **<u>Open with default calendar</u>**:

     - This option will open the default calendar application on your device (e.g., laptop, desktop) and add the ProofHub calendar automatically.

   - **<u>Copy subscriber link for this project</u>**:

     - Choose this to copy the ProofHub calendar link, which you can then add to your preferred calendar tool manually.

---

## **Download a calendar**



<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/86-1.png" width="1440" height="749" alt="" />

1. **<u>Open the context menu</u>**: Click on the three dots at the top right corner of the calendar view.

2. **<u>Select download</u>**: From the context menu, select the “**Download**” option.

   - **<u>iCal for this project</u>**:

     - Download the calendar data in iCal format, suitable for importing into various calendar applications.

   - **<u>CSV for this project</u>**:

     - Download the calendar data in CSV format, useful for spreadsheet applications or custom integrations.

   - **<u>PDF format</u>**:

     - Download a printable version of the calendar data in PDF format.
