---
title: How to archive and unarchive task lists
---
## **Archive a task list**

1. **<u>Select the task list</u>**<u>:</u> Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the task list and select “**Archive**“.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.29.53-PM.png" width="759" height="753" />

2. **<u>Confirm archive:</u>** Select “**Yes**” to confirm and proceed with archiving the task list.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/at2.png" width="594" height="230" alt="" />

---

## **Unarchive a task list**

1. **<u>Navigate to the tasks section:</u>** Open the specific project where the archived task list is located and click on the **“Tasks”** section.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.32.33-PM.png" width="757" height="756" />

2. **<u>Apply task list filter:</u>** Locate the filter icon next to the “**Tasks**” section name. Click on the filter icon and select “**Archived**“.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.36.27-PM.png" width="757" height="754"/>

3. **<u>Unarchive task lists:</u>** Once the filter is applied, the archived tasks list will be displayed. Right-click or click the three-dots icon and select “**Unarchive**“.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.37.53-PM.png" width="760" height="753"/>
