---
title: How to manage workflow in tasks
---
1. **<u>Accessing task workflow</u>**<u>:</u>

   - Select the task list and at the right next to the “**Table view**” option and select “**Workflow**“.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-04-at-4.15.43-PM.png" width="1440" height="753" alt="" />

2. **<u>Editing workflow stages:</u>**


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-10.17.17-AM.png" width="718" height="299" alt="" />

   - In the Workflow edit window, you’ll see options to edit the workflow stages.

   - Enter the name of the new stage and choose a color to represent it.

   - **Change stage color**: Click on the color next to a stage name to change its color.

   - **Rename stage**: Rename stage.

   - **Delete stage**: Hover over a stage, click on three dots, and select “**Delete**“.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-10.18.48-AM.png" width="891" height="298" alt="" />

3. **<u>Apply changes:</u>**

   - Once you’ve made the necessary changes to your workflow stages (adding, renaming, deleting stages, changing colors), simply close the workflow edit window.

   - The task list will instantly reflect the updated workflow with the new stages or modifications.
