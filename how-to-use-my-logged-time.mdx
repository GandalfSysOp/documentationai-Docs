---
title: How to use my logged time
---
To view your logged time entries from all your assigned projects, click on the **“My Logged Time”** option. This feature allows you to record the hours you’ve worked and provides a comprehensive overview of all your time entries in one place. By using this option, you can efficiently manage and review the time you’ve dedicated to various tasks and projects without needing to switch between different project views.\
