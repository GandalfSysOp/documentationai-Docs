---
title: How to download and move notes
---
## **Download a note**

1. **<u>Access download options</u>**<u>:</u> Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the note and select “**Download as PDF**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/2-8.png" width="820" height="613" alt="" />

## **Moving notes to files**

1. **<u>Save in files tab</u>**<u>:</u> Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the note and select “**Save in files tab**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/2-9.png" width="813" height="615" alt="" />

2. **<u>Access the saved PDF</u>**<u>:</u> Go to the files section of the project where you saved the note. Look for the PDF file corresponding to your note.

**<u>Note:</u>** *You won’t be prompted to download the PDF immediately. Instead, the note will be saved as a PDF file in the files section of the project, allowing you to access it later from there. This can be convenient for organizing and storing your notes within ProofHub.*
