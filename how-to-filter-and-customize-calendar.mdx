---
title: How to filter and customize calendar
---
ProofHub’s calendar feature allows you to tailor your view to suit your needs, making it easier to manage your events, milestones, and tasks. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to customize your calendar views effectively:

**Calendar views**\
\



<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/81-1.png" width="1440" height="753" alt="" />

- On the top right corner, just left of the filter option, you will find multiple view options such as day, week, 2 weeks, and month.

- Choose your preferred view by clicking on the desired option (day, week, 2 weeks, or month) to adjust the time scale of your calendar.

---

## **Filter calendar items**

1. **<u>Filtering by calendar items</u>**: To control which calendar items (events, milestones, tasks) are displayed, use the filter option.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/82-1.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

   \


   - Click on the filter icon located near the top right corner of the calendar.

   - In the filter settings, use the “**Show**” option to check or uncheck the types of items you want to display on your calendar (events, milestones, tasks).

   - Simply check or uncheck the boxes next to each item to show/hide them on the calendar.

2. **<u>Filtering by assignee</u>**: You can further refine your calendar view by filtering items for specific assignees.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/83-1.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

   - Within the filter settings, look for the option to filter by the assignee.

   - Choose the assignee whose calendar items you want to display by checking their names in the assignee filter list.

---

## **Navigate by months**

1. Click the “**\<**” (left arrow) to move to the previous month and the “**>**” (right arrow) to move to the next month.

   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/84-1.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

2. Using the date picker option, you can jump to any specific date quickly.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Date-picker-in-calendar.png" width="1322" height="504" alt="Date picker in calendar in ProofHub" />

3. The **“Today”** feature helps you quickly navigate to the current date.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Today-in-calendar.png" width="1327" height="502" alt="Today in calendar in ProofHub" />
