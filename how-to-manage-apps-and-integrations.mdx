title: How to manage apps and integrations
---
ProofHub offers a variety of built-in apps and integrations with third-party tools to enhance your project management experience.

## **Default apps**

ProofHub includes several built-in apps that facilitate various aspects of project management:

1. **Gantt:** Visualize project timelines and track progress using Gantt. Plan and schedule tasks with dependencies.

2. **Notes:** Create, organize, and share notes for quick reference and collaboration.

3. **Files:** Store, organize, and share files within your projects. Manage versions and collaborate on documents.

4. **Time:** Track time spent on tasks and projects. Generate timesheets and reports for better time management.

5. **Reports:** Generate detailed reports on project progress, team performance, and more to make informed decisions.

6. **One-to-one chat:** Communicate directly with team members through instant messaging.

7. **Group chat:** Facilitate team communication with group chats for collaborative discussions.

---

## **Integrations**

ProofHub integrates with various third-party applications to streamline your workflow and enhance productivity:

1. **FreshBooks:** Integrate with FreshBooks for seamless invoicing and expense tracking.

2. **QuickBooks:** Sync with QuickBooks to manage accounting and financials efficiently.

3. **Google Calendar and iCal:** Sync ProofHub with Google Calendar and iCal to keep track of your schedules and deadlines.

4. **Box:** Integrate with Box to manage and share large files securely.

5. **Dropbox:** Connect with Dropbox for easy file sharing and storage.

6. **OneDrive Personal:** Sync with OneDrive for seamless file management and sharing.

---

## **Install and uninstall apps**

1. **Manage section**: Select the “**Manage”** section and select “**Apps/Integrations**“.\

2. To install an app, click on the “**Install”** button next to the app.\

3. To uninstall an app, click on the “**Uninstall”** button next to the app you want to remove.\

## **Disable sections**

1. Locate the section you want to disable.

2. Toggle the switch button to turn off the section.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-12.29.50-PM.png" width="1440" height="753" alt="" />

3. To re-enable a previously disabled section, toggle the switch button to turn on the section.
