---
title: How to cancel an account
---
Only the account owner and those with account cancellation permissions can cancel the account. If you are certain that you need to cancel your account, please follow the steps below:

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Account/billing**” section and select “**Manage plan**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/1-2.png" width="596" height="751" alt="" />

2. **<u>Authentication required</u>**<u>:</u> Type in your current password to proceed.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/2-1.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

3. Scroll to the bottom and click the “**Cancel subscription”** from the bottom right.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-11.09.45-AM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

4. Select the reason for the account cancellation or share your suggestions/feedback with us.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-11.11.19-AM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

   \


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-11.12.07-AM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

5. Click on “**Cancel account**” button.
