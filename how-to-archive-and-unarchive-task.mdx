title: How to archive and unarchive task
---
## **Archive a task**

1. **<u>Select the task:</u>** Right-click on the task name that you want to archive and select “**Archive**“.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.40.25-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="Archive a task in ProofHub" />

2. **<u>Confirm archive:</u>** Select “**Yes**” to confirm and proceed with archiving the task.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-15-at-5.19.42-PM.png" width="598" height="234" alt="" />

---

## **Unarchive a task**

1. **<u>Select the task:</u>** Click on the task list where the archived task is located. Click on the filter icon at the top to reveal filter options.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.42.05-PM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="Unarchive a task in ProofHub" />


   - Within the filter options, locate and select the “**Status**” filter.

   - Choose the “**Archived**” option from the status menu.

   - Click “**Apply**” to filter the task list and display only archived tasks.

2. **<u>Unarchive the task:</u>** Once the filters are applied, archived tasks will be displayed.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.45.08-PM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="Unarchive a task in ProofHub" />

   - Right-click on the task name and select “**Unarchive**“.

   - Select “**Yes**” to confirm.
