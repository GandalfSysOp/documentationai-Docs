## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
  <summary><strong>Do I need to purchase a license for signing up with ProofHub?</strong></summary>

  There is no need to purchase any kind of license for signing up with us. All that you require is an internet connection and a modern web browser.
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>How can I log in to my account?</strong></summary>

  All you need to do is type your account URL into the web browser and enter your login credentials.

  If login fails, contact support@proofhub.com
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>What browsers does ProofHub support?</strong></summary>

  We support the latest stable versions of:

  - Chrome
  - Safari
  - Firefox
  - Edge
  - Chromium
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>Do I get limited features in the trial period?</strong></summary>

  No, you get access to all the features of ProofHub during the trial.
</details>

<details>
  <summary><strong>What payment methods do you accept?</strong></summary>

  We accept:
  
  - PayPal
  - Visa
  - Mastercard
  - American Express
</details>
