---
title: Introduction to calendar
---
ProofHub calendar is designed to streamline your project management experience. With the calendar feature, you can easily add tasks, events, and milestones, allowing you to stay organized and focused on key project deliverables.

By syncing the ProofHub calendar with iCal or Google Calendar, you can easily include project timelines with your current workflow and stay up to date with your schedule at all times.

Organize and manage significant occasions and project checkpoints effectively, fostering effective teamwork and communication.

1. **<u>Event</u>**: Easily organize and plan significant occasions, ensuring that everyone is aware of them and coordinated.

2. **<u>Milestone</u>**: Establish project milestones to identify significant accomplishments and track advancement toward objectives.

3. **<u>Task</u>**: To ensure clarity and attention on project deliverables, add and prioritize tasks directly on the calendar.
