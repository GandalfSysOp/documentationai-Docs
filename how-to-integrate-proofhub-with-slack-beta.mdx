---
title: How to integrate ProofHub with Slack (Beta)
---
Monitor task progress, update assignees, set due dates, and ensure timely delivery using ProofHub’s integration with Slack – all from one platform.\


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Slack-1.png" width="768" height="333" alt="" />

### **Install Slack**

1. **<u>Navigate to the manage section:</u>** Click on the gear icon on the left side. Select “**Manage > Apps/integrations”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Slack-1-1.png" width="649" height="307" alt="" />

2. A window will open displaying all available apps for integration. Click on “Install” under Slack to enable it.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Slack-2.png" width="788" height="759" alt="" />

## **Configure Slack with ProofHub’s project**

1. **<u>Configure setting</u>**<u>:</u> Choose the specific project within ProofHub that you want to integrate with Slack and select the gear icon next to the project’s name and click on **“Integrations”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Configure-slack-with-ProofHub-Project.png" width="588" height="452" alt="Configure slack with ProofHub Project" />

2. Click on the **“Next”** option.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/slack-4.png" width="580" height="259" alt="" />

3. Click on **“Connect“** to send project notifications to the Slack channel.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Slack-5.png" width="586" height="199" alt="" />

4. **<u>Enter Slack details:</u>** You will be redirected to the new window. Here enter your workspace Slack URL and click on continue.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Slack-6.png" width="768" height="506" alt="" />



5. Further, enter the email address and password for your Slack account.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/slack-7.png" width="840" height="593" alt="" />



6. **<u>Authorization:</u>** Grant access to your Slack account for ProofHub by clicking **“Allow”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/slack-9.png" width="1104" height="634" alt="" />

7. **<u>Select channels:</u>** You can select the channel where you want to receive notifications for project updates. Additionally, you can choose the notifications you would like to receive related to the tasks.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/slack-7-1.png" width="769" height="492" alt="" />

## **Connect ProofHub to Slack**

Use **“/proofhub connect”** to authenticate and connect ProofHub to Slack.\


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Slack-9-1.png" width="1013" height="485" alt="" />

## **Create new tasks in Slack**

1. Use **“/proofhub add task”** to create a new task.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/slack-10.png" width="719" height="449" alt="" />

2. Select a project, write 3 letters of your project, and click on Add. (minimum 3 letters)\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Slack-11.png" width="608" height="249" alt="" />

3. Enter the task details and click on **“Add”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/slack-12.png" width="506" height="624" alt="" />

4. The task has been added.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/slack-13.png" width="888" height="265" alt="" />

## **Use the context menu to update tasks**

1. Select edit task, to update the dates, assignees, and other details of the task.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Slack-14.png" width="1024" height="497" alt="" />

2. To add comments on tasks, select “**Add comment”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Slack-15.png" width="1024" height="469" alt="" />

3. You can archive the task and mark the task as completed.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Slack-16.png" width="1024" height="310" alt="" />

   \


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/slack-17.png" width="1024" height="406" alt="" />

**<u>Note:</u>****&#xA0;***Currently, we do not support private channels in Slack.*
