---
title: How to manage bulk allocation
---
The bulk allocation feature in ProofHub allows you to efficiently assign and subscribe multiple tasks, files, discussions, and notes from one person to another in a single action.

## **Bulk allocate items**

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Manage”** section and select “**Bulk allocation**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-12.06.29-PM.png" width="597" height="756" alt="" />

2. **<u>Manage selections</u>**<u>:</u>\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-12.14.02-PM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

   - **<u>Assigning/subscribing items to:</u>**

     - **Definition:** This refers to the person who will receive the selected items (tasks, files, discussions, notes).

     - **Action:** Select the user from the dropdown menu or list to whom you want to assign or subscribe to these items.

   - **<u>Assigning/subscribing items of:</u>**

     - **Definition:** This refers to the person whose items (tasks, files, discussions, notes) will be reassigned or have a new subscriber added.

     - **Action:** Select the user from the dropdown menu or list whose items you want to bulk allocate.

   - **<u>Projects:</u>**

     - **Definition:** These are the projects within which the items to be reassigned or subscribed are located.

     - **Action:** Choose the specific project(s) where the bulk allocation should take place. This ensures that only items within these projects are affected.

   - **<u>Select items:</u>**

     - **Definition:** These are the specific items (tasks, files, discussions, notes) that will be reassigned or subscribed.

     - **Action:** Select the items you want to include in the bulk allocation. You can choose individual items or select all items within the chosen projects.

   - **<u>Select bulk action</u>**

     - **Replace current assignee/subscriber:** This option will replace the current person assigned or subscribed to the selected items with the new person.

     - **Add new assignee/subscriber:** This option will add the new person as an additional assignee or subscriber without removing the current ones.

3. Click on the **“Apply”** button.
