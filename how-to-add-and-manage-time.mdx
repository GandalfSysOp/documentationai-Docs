## **Add a time entry**

1. **<u>Select a timesheet</u>****:** Open the timesheet where you would like to add the time.

2. **<u>Add time</u>****:** Click on the “**Add**” button.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/101.png" width="690" height="392" alt="" />

3. **<u>Enter time details</u>****:**\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Enter-time-details.png" width="709" height="741" alt="Enter time details in ProofHub" />

   - **<u>Hours</u>****:** Enter the hours and minutes in the designated column.

   - **<u>Description</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x45;nter the description in the description fiel&#x64;*.*

   - **<u>Status</u>****:** You can define a status for your time entry for example billed, billable, non-billable, void, or none.

   - **<u>Associate a task:</u>****&#xA0;**&#x49;f the time entry is directly related to a specific task, enable the “**Add Time Details to Task**” option, then select the appropriate **“Tasklist**” and **‘Task.**“

4. **<u>Save time</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x43;lick the **“Add”** button to save your time.

**<u>Note:</u>** *For more on how to log time over a task&#x20;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/tasks/how-to-log-time-in-a-task" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*click here.*</a>

## **Sort and filter time entries**

The sort and filter options enable users to effortlessly organize and access time entries based on various parameters, facilitating streamlined time tracking and performance analysis.\
\


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/107.png" width="1440" height="358" alt="" />

1. **<u>Date range:</u>** By default, the **“All”&#xA0;**&#x6F;ption is selected. You can select different date ranges to view data from different time ranges.

2. **<u>Status</u>****:** All the time entries are available to view, you can use the filter to view time entries by different statuses like billable, billed, or non-billable.

3. **<u>Logged by</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x59;ou can use the logged by filter to view logged time entries by users.

4. **<u>Group by</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x54;ime entries can be grouped by date, month, person, tasklist.

5. **<u>Order by</u>****:** Choose between **“Asc”&#x20;**(ascending) and **“Desc”** (descending) to arrange your time entries by the date logged.

## **Timer**

In ProofHub, a timer is a tool that enables users to track the duration spent on specific tasks or projects. It allows you to start, pause, resume, and stop timing activities, providing accurate records of time spent for better project management and billing purposes.

1. **<u>Timer from task</u>****:** Click on **“Add logged time”**,**&#xA0;**&#x61;n&#x64;**&#xA0;**&#x73;elect **“Use timer”.**\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/108.png" width="727" height="570" alt="" />



2. Once the timer has started, it will appear in the respective task and also in the multiple-timer window.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/109.png" width="729" height="574" alt="" />



3. In case a new timer is started then the existing running timer will be paused.

4. To save the time tracked by a running or paused timer, you need to hover over it in the task window and click on **“Save time”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/110.png" width="783" height="567" alt="" />



5. **&#xA0;****<u>Add time</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x41; window will open in which you can add all necessary details like timesheet, description, status, tasklist, and task.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/111.png" width="698" height="726" alt="" />

## **View all paused, running, and global timers**

1. **<u>View all paused and running timers</u>****:**\
   \
   Click on the clock icon on the right-side menu.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/112.png" width="978" height="451" alt="" />

   \
   \
   When you access the list of timers, you’ll find both running timers and those on hold. It allows you to easily resume your work or restart a timer from the paused ones.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/113.png" width="992" height="398" alt="" />



2. **<u>Starting the timer quickly</u>**:**&#x20;using the global timer**\
   ProofHub empowers you to begin tracking your time efficiently without navigating through projects and tasks. Here’s how to utilize the global timer option:

   - **<u>Access the global timer</u>****:** A clock icon on the right-side menu represents this option.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/114.png" width="978" height="451" alt="" />



   - **<u>Start tracking time</u>****:** Click on the global timer and select **“Start a timer”**. Your timer will begin running, logging the duration of your work.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/115.png" width="1434" height="786" alt="" />



   - **<u>Stop and save time</u>**: Once you’ve completed your work, click the **“Save Time”** button to stop the timer and record the logged duration.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/116.png" width="1439" height="466" alt="" />



   - **<u>Add details</u>****:** After saving your time entry, a window will appear where you can allocate the logged time to a specific project, timesheet. You can also add a description, set the status, and assign it to an individual task within the project\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/117.png" width="700" height="553" alt="" />

- **<u>Note:</u>**

  - *With each timer, you’ll get information about the item(s) on which it is running.*

  - *On hovering over a timer, you’ll get the&#xA0;****“Save time”****&#xA0;option, which you can click to save the time tracked in a timesheet.*

  - *If a timer is started through the task itself, then only the task information will be seen on the timer.*
