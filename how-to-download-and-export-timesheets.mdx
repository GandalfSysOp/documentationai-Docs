title: How to download and export timesheets
---
## **Download timesheet**

ProofHub offers a convenient export function that allows you to download your timesheets in a readily usable format. This empowers you to leverage your time data for various purposes.\


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/149.png" width="1434" height="465" alt="" />

1. **<u>Access your timesheets</u>****:** Navigate to the **“Time”** section. You’ll see a list of all timesheets.

2. **<u>Select the desired timesheet</u>****:** Click on the three dots situated next to the filters icon at the top of the selected timesheet. 

3. **<u>Choose export option</u>****:** Select the **Export as CSV** option.

---

## **Export timesheet**

You can export timesheets with just a few clicks, providing you with the flexibility to utilize them in Freshbooks, Quickbooks, or a standard CSV file.

1. **<u>Select the timesheet</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x46;ind the timesheet and click the three dots or right-click.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/150.png" width="461" height="605" alt="" />



2. **<u>Select “Export”</u>****:** This will display a popup window with three choices: Freshbooks, Quickbooks, and CSV.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/151.png" width="584" height="432" alt="" />

3. **<u>Select your export option</u>****.** Select the option that best suits your needs:

   - **Freshbooks or Quickbooks:** If you use either of these accounting platforms, selecting them will prompt you to log in to your account and directly export the timesheet data.

   - **CSV:** When you choose CSV, all entries in the timesheet will be included in the downloaded file.
