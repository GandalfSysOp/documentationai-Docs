---
title: How to set a background image
---
Customizing your workspace with a background image in ProofHub can make your experience more pleasant and personalized.

1. **Access the background image settings**:

   - Navigate to the bottom left corner and click on the “**Set background image**” option.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-4.48.39-PM.png" width="445" height="754" alt="" />

2. **Search for a desired image**:

   - Use the search bar to find an image that suits your taste or the theme of your workspace.

   - Enter keywords related to the type of image you want (e.g., nature, abstract, office).\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-4.49.42-PM.png" width="496" height="754" alt="" />

3. **Select default images**:

   - ProofHub provides a selection of default images you can choose from.

   - Browse these images and click on the one you like to set as your background.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-4.50.43-PM.png" width="492" height="754" alt="" />

4. **Set to change randomly**:

   - If you prefer variety, select the option to have your background image change randomly.

   - This will rotate through a collection of images, keeping your workspace fresh and dynamic.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-4.51.35-PM.png" width="495" height="753" alt="" />

5. **Set a white background**:

   - For a calming and professional look, select the option for a blue background.

   - This is a solid color option that provides a simple and clean appearance.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-4.52.35-PM.png" width="486" height="753" alt="" />

6. **Blur background using the slider**:

   - If you want to reduce distractions while still having a visually appealing background, use the blur slider to adjust the blurriness of the background image.

   - Move the slider to the right to increase the blur effect, or to the left to decrease it, until you achieve the desired level of clarity or blur.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-4.56.08-PM.png" width="497" height="752" alt="" />
