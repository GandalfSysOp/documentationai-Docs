## **Introduction to forms**

ProofHub forms provide a streamlined way to gather requests from internal users and external stakeholders, enabling efficient communication and task creation within projects. Submitted requests through forms are automatically converted into tasks and added to a selected task list, facilitating organized task management and workflow. *No ProofHub account is required to submit a form.*

## **Add a form from the task section**

1. Right-click or click the three-dots icon and select “**Form**“.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-9.25.23-PM.png" width="757" height="753" alt="How to add forms in ProofHub" />

2. **<u>Enter form title and description:</u>** Enter the title and description and click “**Next**” to proceed to the form customization options.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-4.02.41-PM.png" width="712" height="290" alt="" />

3. **<u>Customize form fields:</u>**


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-4.04.17-PM.png" width="716" height="712" alt="" />

   - Select which custom fields from the selected task list you want to display in the form.

   - Note that the Name, Email, and Title fields are mandatory.

   - Control field visibility by clicking on “**Included in form**” or toggling the eye icon next to each field.

   - Mark fields as mandatory if necessary by enabling the mandatory “**Required**” option.

   - Enable the “**Allow attachments**” option if you want people to be able to upload files along with their form submissions.
     **<u>Note:</u>***&#x20;Users can upload up to 5 files, each with a maximum size of 10 MB.*

4. **<u>Customize form appearance:</u>**


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-4.09.21-PM.png" width="712" height="642" alt="" />

   - Customize the visual appearance of the form by selecting background color, text color, button color, and button text color.

   - Define the success message that will be displayed after successful form submission.

   - Choose whether users can submit multiple requests (resubmissions) or redirect users to a specific URL after form submission.

   - Automatically assign specific labels to tasks created via this form by selecting the desired labels.

   - Enable or disable CAPTCHA by the “**Add captcha** **to form**” option.

## **Add a form from the manage section**

1. Click on the “**Manage**” icon located in the bottom left corner. Select “**Forms**” from the Manage section.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-9.35.42-PM.png" width="1440" height="756" alt="Add a form from the manage section" />

2. On the forms screen, click on the “**Add**” option to create a new form.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-3.47.53-PM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

3. In the form creation window, provide the necessary details:


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-3.04.42-PM.png" width="716" height="440" alt="" />

   - **Project**: Select the project to which the form will be associated.

   - **Task list:&#x20;**&#x43;hoose the task lis&#x74;**&#x20;**&#x77;here tasks created from form submissions will be added.

   - **Form title**: Enter a title for your form.

   - **Form description**: Describe your form.

   - Click “**Next”** to proceed.
