---
title: How to add and manage project statuses
---
Project statuses in ProofHub help you track the progress and current state of your projects. Follow these steps to manage your project statuses:

## **Add a project status**

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Manage”** section and select “**Project statuses**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.08.12-PM.png" width="600" height="754" alt="" />

2. **<u>Add a new project status</u>**<u>:</u>\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.15.56-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="" />

   - Click the **“Add”** button.

   - Enter a title and click **“Add”**.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.24.38-PM.png" width="712" height="188" alt="" />

---

## **Edit a project status**

1. Right-click or click on the three dots and select “**Edit**”.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.16.35-PM-1.png" width="1043" height="332" alt="" />

2. From the edit window make changes as required and click “**Update**“.

   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.17.26-PM-1.png" width="716" height="185" alt="" />

**<u>Note:</u>** *“Active” and “Archived” project statuses are system defaults and cannot be edited.*

---

## **Delete a project status**

1. Right-click or click on the three dots and select **“Delete**”.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.17.51-PM.png" width="1039" height="280" alt="" />

2. Associate an alternative project status and select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to proceed with deletion.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.18.54-PM.png" width="497" height="313" alt="" />

**<u>Note:</u>** *“Active” and “Archived” project statuses are system defaults and cannot be deleted.* *ProofHub keeps items in the “Trash” for 15 days. Restore them easily by going to Trash. For detailed instructions on restoring deleted items from the trash, please refer to&#x20;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/manage/how-to-manage-trash" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*Trash*</a>*.*

---

## **Create a copy of the project status**

1. Right-click or click on the three dots and select **“Create a copy**”.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.19.13-PM.png" width="1044" height="284" alt="" />

2. Enter a new title as required and click “**Ok”** to save.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.20.14-PM.png" width="719" height="185" alt="" />
