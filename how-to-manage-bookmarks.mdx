---
title: How to manage bookmarks
---
ProofHub’s Bookmark feature allows you to quickly access important task lists, notes, and other items by bookmarking them. This guide will walk you through how to access, add, and delete bookmarks efficiently.

## **Bookmarks**

Go to the right-side menu and click on the “**Bookmarks**” option. This will display a list of all your bookmarked items for quick access.\

## **Delete a bookmark**

Open the “**Bookmarks**” section from the right-side menu to see all your bookmarked items. Click on the three dots and select “**Delete**“.\
