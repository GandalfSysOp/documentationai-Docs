---
title: How to change views in tasks
---
ProofHub offers two main views for managing your tasks: Table view and Board view. Each view provides a different way of organizing and visualizing your tasks, catering to various project management needs.

## **Table view**

The table view presents tasks in a tabular format, similar to a spreadsheet. This view displays tasks in rows with columns for different attributes like task name, assignee, due date, status, etc.

Users who prefer a structured, detailed, and linear approach to task management. It’s ideal for tracking and sorting tasks based on various criteria.

## **Board view**

The board view uses a kanban-style layout, where tasks are represented as cards organized into columns. Each column typically represents a different stage of the workflow (e.g., To Do, In Progress, Done).

Users who favor a visual, drag-and-drop interface for managing tasks. It’s great for quickly understanding the status of tasks and moving them through different stages of completion.

## **Change view**

At the top of the “**Tasks**” section, you will see options to switch between views select **“Table view**” or “**Board view**” as per your preference.
