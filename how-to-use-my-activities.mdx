---
title: How to use my activities
---
To view your activities across all assigned projects, click on the **“My Activities”** option. This feature provides a detailed overview of all your actions and contributions within each project, consolidating them into one comprehensive view. By using this option, you can easily track your progress, review your involvement in different tasks, and ensure that you stay on top of all your project-related activities without needing to navigate through each individual project.\
