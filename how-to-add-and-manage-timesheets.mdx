title: How to add and manage timesheets
---
## **Add a timesheet**

1. **<u>Navigate to the time section</u>****:** Select the specific project where you want to create the timesheet. Click the **“Time”** tab on the left-side menu.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/121.png" width="558" height="320" alt="" />



2. **<u>Create a new timesheet</u>****:** Click on the **“+”** icon to initiate the creation of a new timesheet.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/122.png" width="432" height="391" alt="" />



3. **<u>Enter timesheet details</u>****:** Enter a title and other details for your timesheet in the provided fields.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/123.png" width="698" height="387" alt="" />

- **<u>Estimated time</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x59;ou can provide an estimate for the timesheet in the given column.

- **<u>Private timesheet</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x49;f you want to keep this timesheet as private, then check the option “**Mark as private**” and select “**Subscribers**” with whom you wish to share the timesheet.

&#x34;**.&#x20;****<u>Save the timesheet</u>****:** After entering the details, click the **“Add”** button to save your new timesheet.

---

## **Edit a timesheet**

1. Click the three-dots icon or right-click on the timesheet and select “**Edit**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/edit-time-sheet.png" width="546" height="522" alt="" />



2. **<u>Edit the details</u>****:&#xA0;**&#xA0;Modify the details of the timesheet title or **“Mark as private”**. After making changes, click “**Update**” to save.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/125.png" width="707" height="240" alt="" />

---

## **Delete a timesheet**

1. Click the three-dots icon or right-click on the timesheet and select “**Delete**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Delete-timesheet-1.png" width="546" height="522" alt="" />

2. **<u>Delete:</u>** Select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to delete.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/127.png" width="436" height="200" alt="" />

**<u>Note: </u>***ProofHub keeps items in the “Trash” for 15 days. Restore them easily by going to Trash. For detailed instructions on restoring deleted items from the trash, please refer to&#xA0;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/manage/how-to-manage-trash" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*Trash*</a>*.*

---

## **Archive a timesheet**

1. Click the three-dots icon or right-click on the timesheet and select “**Archive**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Archive-timesheet.png" width="546" height="522" alt="" />

2. **<u>Confirm archive</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x53;elect “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to archive.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/129.png" width="580" height="214" alt="" />

---

## **Unarchive a timesheet**

1. **<u>Navigate to the time section</u>****:** Select the specific project and click the **“Time”** tab on the left-side menu.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/130.png" width="558" height="320" alt="" />



2. **<u>Use filter</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x43;lick on the filter to toggle from active to archived timesheets.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/131.png" width="619" height="431" alt="" />



3. **<u>Select timesheet</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x4F;n the archived timesheet, click the three dots, or right-click to select **“Unarchive”**\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/unarchive-timesheet.png" width="520" height="477" alt="" />

---

## **Bookmark a timesheet**

1. Click the three-dots icon or right-click on the timesheet and select “**Bookmark**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/bookmark-timesheet.png" width="546" height="522" alt="" />



2. The bookmarked timesheet will appear in the right-side menu with other saved bookmarks.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/access-bookmarked-timesheet.png" width="398" height="311" alt="" />
