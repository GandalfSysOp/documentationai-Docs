---
title: How to utilize all calendars
---
ProofHub’s “**All calendars**” consolidates all calendar items, including events, milestones, and tasks across different projects assigned to anyone, into a single, unified view.

## **All calendars**

1. Click on “**Everything**” and select the **“All calendars”** option.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-5.53.02-PM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

2. In the “**All calendars**” view, you will see all calendar items i.e. events, milestones, and tasks across different projects.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-5.55.51-PM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

---

## **Filters**

To tailor your calendar view according to specific criteria, you can use the filter options:

1. **<u>Access filters</u>**<u>:</u> Click on the filter icon located at the top right corner of the “**All calendars**” page.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-3.23.30-PM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

2. **<u>Available filters</u>**<u>:</u>

   - **Project**: Select specific projects to view their associated calendar items.

   - **Show**: Choose to display events, milestones, and tasks.

   - **Assigned**: Filter by assigned to see calendar items relevant to specific team members.

---

## **Subscribe**

Staying updated with calendar changes is easy with the subscription options, click on the three dots next to the filter icon and select “**Subscribe**“.\


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-3.27.40-PM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

1. **<u>Open with default calendar</u>**: Choose this option to integrate the calendar with your default calendar application directly.

2. **<u>Copy subscribe link for all projects</u>****:** Use this option to get a subscription link that you can add to any calendar application that supports external calendars.

---

## **Download**

You can download the calendar for offline access or sharing purposes in various formats. Click on the three dots next to the filter icon and select “**Download**“.\


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-3.28.37-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="" />

1. **iCal**: Suitable for importing into most calendar applications.

2. **CSV**: Useful for spreadsheet applications and data analysis.

3. **PDF**: Ideal for printing and sharing static calendar views.
