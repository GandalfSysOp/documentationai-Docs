---
title: How to add and manage workflow
---
ProofHub allows you to manage your existing workflows, create new workflows from scratch, and create copies of existing workflows.

## **Add a new workflow**

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Manage”** section and select “**Workflows**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-11.40.31-AM.png" width="599" height="755" alt="" />

2. **<u>Add a new workflow:</u>**\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-11.42.59-AM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

   - Click the **“Add”** button.

   - Enter a name for your new workflow and click **“Add”**.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.20.57-PM.png" width="717" height="186" alt="" />

---

## **Edit a workflow**

1. **<u>Add new stages</u>**<u>:</u> Add new stages to the workflow as needed.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-11.45.04-AM.png" width="1035" height="210" alt="" />

2. **<u>Edit existing stages</u>**<u>:</u> Modify the names and details of existing stages.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-11.46.47-AM.png" width="1044" height="253" alt="" />

3. **<u>Change color for stages</u>**<u>:</u> Customize the color for each stage to help visually distinguish them.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-11.48.40-AM.png" width="1043" height="303" alt="" />

---

## **Create a copy of the workflow**

1. Right-click or click on the three dots and select **“Create a copy**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-11.58.21-AM.png" width="1039" height="349" alt="" />

2. Enter a new name(if required) and click “**Ok”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-11.59.59-AM.png" width="717" height="185" alt="" />

---

## **Delete a workflow**

1. Right-click or click on the three dots and select **“Delete**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.03.57-PM.png" width="1038" height="381" alt="" />

2. Associate an alternative workflow and select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to proceed with the deletion.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.06.25-PM.png" width="495" height="366" alt="" />

**Note:** *Default workflows cannot be deleted.* *ProofHub keeps items in the “Trash” for 15 days. Restore them easily by going to Trash. For detailed instructions on restoring deleted items from the trash, please refer to&#xA0;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/manage/how-to-manage-trash" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*Trash*</a>*.*
