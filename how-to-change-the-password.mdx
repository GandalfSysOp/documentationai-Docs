---
title: How to change the password
---
In ProofHub, if you have the required access, you can assist users in changing or updating their passwords. Users with owner access or custom access to the People section can help other users with their passwords.

1. **Navigate to the people section:** Click on the three dots or right-click on the user name.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/change_password.png" width="1375" height="559" alt="" />

2. From the context menu that appears, click on **“Change Password”**.

3. **Enter the new password**: In the new window, type the new password. Save the new password by clicking the **“Update”** button.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Change-password-2.png" width="707" height="229" alt="" />

   \
   **Note:** *The password must be at least 5 characters long.*
