---
title: How to upload files via email-in
---
ProofHub allows you to upload files via email too.\
\


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Upload-files-via-email-in.png" width="1308" height="594" alt="Upload files via email-in in ProofHub's files section" />

1. Right-click on the folder or click on the three dots and select “**Copy email-in address”**. The email-in address will be copied to the clipboard and you can save it in your emailing system.

2. Compose an email, paste the email-in address in the recipient field and attach the file to that email.

3. Clic&#x6B;**&#xA0;“Send**” and you will be able to upload files via email.
