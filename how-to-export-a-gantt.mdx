---
title: How to export a gantt
---
There are two ways you can export the gantt in ProofHub.\
\


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Export-1.png" width="1363" height="407" alt="" />

1. Click on the three dots icon at the top-right of the page.

2. Select “**Print”&#xA0;**&#x74;o print the gantt chart.

3. Or else, download the gantt chart in PDF format by choosing the “**Export as PDF**” option. 

**<u>Note:</u>****&#xA0;***If you can’t fit the gantt chart on* one screen while exporting/printing, then try changing the settings as follow&#x73;*:*

- *Change the DPI to “****200***“

- *Select the orientation to “****Landscape***“

- Select the paper format to “**A3**“
