---
title: Bulk actions on people
---
ProofHub allows you to perform bulk actions on your team members, such as assigning roles, moving them to groups, suspending accounts, deleting users, and resending invitations.

1. On the left-side menu, navigate to the people section.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/people-bulk.png" width="565" height="402" alt="" />

2. **<u>Identify and select users</u>****:** Hover over any user’s name to reveal the checkboxes, and check these boxes to select the people for a bulk action. You can choose specific names or select everyone at once by checking the **“Select all”** check box at the top.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/People-bulk-2.png" width="1440" height="788" alt="" />

3. **<u>Manage users in bulk:</u>** After the selection, you can right-click on any person or use the bulk actions menu. This will display a menu with several options.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/06/people-bulk-3-1.png" width="1435" height="777" alt="" />

   - **Assign role:&#x20;**&#x4D;odify the role of selected people.

   - **Move to group:** This options helps you to move selected users to a group.

   - **Suspend:** To temporarily restrict access for selected people, use the **“Suspend”&#x20;**&#x66;unction.

   - **Delete:** Remove selected users from your account.

   - **Resend invite:** You can resend the invites to the selected users who haven’t logged in or are yet to be verified.

**<u>Note: </u>***ProofHub keeps items in the “Trash” for 15 days. Restore them easily by going to Trash. For detailed instructions on restoring deleted items from the trash, please refer to&#xA0;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/manage/how-to-manage-trash" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*Trash*</a>*.*
