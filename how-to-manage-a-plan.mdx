title: How to manage a plan
---
Managing your ProofHub subscription plan is straightforward and allows you to adjust your billing cycle and choose the plan that best fits your needs.

## **Manage plan**

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Account/billing”** section and select “**Manage plan**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-4.55.03-PM.png" width="596" height="751" alt="" />

2. **<u>Authentication required</u>**<u>:</u> Type in your current password to proceed.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-12.44.16-PM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

3. **<u>Selecting your billing cycle:</u>** You will be given two options for the billing cycle: **Monthly** or **Annually**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-12.50.34-PM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

4. Click on “**Pick me**” to proceed further.

## **Storage**

ProofHub offers cloud storage for your projects, allowing you to:

- **Centralize files:** Store all project-related documents, images, and videos in one place, accessible to your team.

- **Easy collaboration:** Team members can upload, share, and collaborate on files directly within ProofHub, eliminating the need for scattered storage solutions.

- **Version control:** Track changes made to files and revert to previous versions if needed.

This centralized storage streamlines project communication and keeps everything organized for your team.

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Account/billing”** section and select “**Storage**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-4.56.26-PM.png" width="595" height="753" alt="" />

2. **<u>Review available storage options:</u>** You will see a list of available storage capacities with different pricing.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-1.11.34-PM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

## **Payment and billing**

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Account/billing”** section and select “**Payment/billing**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-4.59.26-PM.png" width="595" height="753" alt="" />

2. **<u>Managing billing address:</u>**\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-1.38.00-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="" />

   - Click on the “**Update”** button next to the billing address section.

   - Fill in the required billing address details, including street address, city, state, postal code, and country.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-1.37.47-PM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

   - Click on the “**Update”** button to save your new billing address.

3. **<u>Managing payment details:</u>**\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-1.38.24-PM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

   Ensure your billing address is up-to-date and correctly entered.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-1.38.58-PM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

   Select your preferred payment method from the available options:\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-1.39.32-PM.png" width="1440" height="753" alt="" />

   - Subscription start option:

     - **End the free trial & start subscription now:** This option will immediately start your paid subscription.

     - **Start subscription after the free trial ends:** This option will start your subscription once your free trial period is over.

4. Click on the **“Subscribe now”** button to start your subscription.
