## **Add a note**

1. **<u>Select a notebook:</u>** Open the notebook where you like to create the note.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/4-1.png" width="571" height="578" alt="" />

   \
   \


2. **<u>Add note:</u>** Click on the “**Add**” button.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/5-1.png" width="1024" height="560" alt="" />

   \
   \


3. **<u>Enter note details:</u>**\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/6-1.png" width="712" height="299" alt="" />

   \


   - **Title:&#xA0;**&#x50;rovide a title for your note in the designated field.

   - **Color:** Customize the color of the note by clicking on the color selector. By setting a color for your note, you can also utilize the color filter to conveniently sort and organize your notes based on color categories.

   - **Subscribers:** If you want specific team members notified about this note, select them from the subscriber list.

   - **Mark as private:** Check the “**Mark as private**” option if you need to restrict the visibility of the note to selected subscribers only.

4. **<u>Save the note:</u>****&#xA0;**&#x43;lick the “**Add**” button to save your note.

---

## **Manage people in notes**

1. **<u>Access manage people</u>**: Click the three-dots icon or right-click on the note and select “**Manage people**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-5.39.46-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="" />

2. **<u>Add or remove subscribers</u>**<u>:</u> Click on the “**Manage People**” option within this window to view a list of people you can manage for this note.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/8-1.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />



3. **<u>Select subscribers</u>**: In the subscribers’ window, you’ll see a list of existing subscribers who have access to this note.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/9-1.png" width="1055" height="753" alt="" />

   - Tick the checkbox next to the names of the people you want to add as subscribers or uncheck to remove them from subscribers and click on the “**Update**” button to save and apply the changes.

---

## **Edit a note**

1. **<u>Select the note:</u>** Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the note and select “**Edit**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Screenshot-2024-05-24-at-1.04.36-PM.png" width="813" height="752" alt="" />

2. **<u>Edit the details:</u>** Modify the note’s title, add or remove a subscriber, or select the “**Mark as private**” option. After making changes, click “**Update**” to save.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/10-2.png" width="715" height="302" alt="" />



---

## **Delete a note**

1. **<u>Select the note</u>**<u>:</u> Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the note and select “**Delete**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/10-3.png" width="819" height="611" alt="" />

2. **<u>Confirm deletion</u>**<u>:</u> Select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to proceed with deleting the note.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/10-4.png" width="333" height="176" alt="" />

---

## **Create a copy of the note**

1. Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the note and select “**Create a copy**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Screenshot-2024-05-24-at-1.02.20-PM.png" width="777" height="752" alt="" />

2. **<u>Create a copy:</u>** This option allows you to duplicate the note. A pop-up window will prompt you to rename the note title(If required), select the project, and the notebook where the copy will be saved.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/9-5.png" width="714" height="416" alt="" />

---

## **Move a note**

1. Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the note and select “**Move**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/9-8.png" width="775" height="752" alt="" />

2. **<u>Move:</u>** Select this option to move the note to another project or notebook. A pop-up window will appear, prompting you to choose the destination project and the notebook.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/7-2.png" width="717" height="411" alt="" />

---

## **Bookmark note**

1. Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the note and select “**Bookmark**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/7-3.png" width="778" height="752" alt="" />

2. The bookmarked note will appear in the right-side menu with other saved bookmarks.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Screenshot-2024-05-24-at-9.28.45-AM.png" width="426" height="380" alt="" />
