---
title: How to add and manage announcements
---
The **Announcements** in ProofHub is a versatile tool for communicating important information that may not be tied to a specific project. You can use it to appreciate a job well done, celebrate achievements, or send birthday greetings.

## **Add announcements**

1. **<u>Add a new announcement</u>**: Click the “**Add**” button.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-3.17.27-PM.png" width="496" height="374" alt="" />

2. **<u>Enter announcement details</u>**:\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-3.18.22-PM.png" width="727" height="564" alt="" />

   - **Title**: Enter a title for your announcement.

   - **Description**: Write the detailed content of your announcement. This could be appreciation for a job well done, celebration of achievements, or birthday greetings. You can add formatting, images, or attachments to make your announcement more engaging.

   - **Subscribers**:

     - Click on the “**+**” icon and select the team members you want to notify about this announcement.

     - You can choose team members individually or select groups.

   - **Keep subscribers hidden**: Enable this option if you want to keep the list of subscribers private.

   - **Lasts for**: Set the duration for which the announcement will be visible. Options include 24 hours, 48 hours, a week, or forever.

   - **Schedule for future**: Schedule the announcement to be posted at a future date and time.

   - **Add files & documents:** Attach any necessary files or documents related to the announcement.

   - **Allow comments**: Enable or disable comments on the announcement.

3. **<u>Publish the announcement</u>**: Click the “**Add**” button to publish your announcement.

**<u>Note:</u>****&#xA0;***If you want this announcement to be easily accessible and visible at the top of the list, consider using the “****Pin to Top****” option.*

---

## **Edit announcements**

1. **<u>Edit an announcement</u>**: Click on the three dots and select “**Edit**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-3.26.43-PM.png" width="609" height="373" alt="" />

2. Make the necessary changes and click on “**Update**” to save the changes.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-3.28.10-PM.png" width="730" height="718" alt="" />

## **Delete announcements**

1. **<u>Delete an announcement</u>**: Click on the three dots and select “**Delete**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-3.29.00-PM.png" width="610" height="375" alt="" />

2. Select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to proceed with the deletion.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-3.30.33-PM.png" width="442" height="175" alt="" />

**<u>Note:</u>** *ProofHub keeps items in the “Trash” for 15 days. Restore them easily by going to Trash. For detailed instructions on restoring deleted items from the trash, please refer to&#xA0;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/manage/how-to-manage-trash" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*Trash*</a>*.*

## **Pin and unpin announcements**

1. Pin the announcement to the top of the list to ensure it remains prominently visible.

2. **<u>Pin to top:</u>** Click on the three dots and select “**Pin to top**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-3.35.38-PM.png" width="615" height="376" alt="" />

3. **<u>Unpin:</u>** Click on the three dots and select “**Unpin**“.\
