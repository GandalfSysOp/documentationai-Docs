<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Do I need to purchase a license for signing up with ProofHub?">
There is no need to purchase any kind of license for signing up with us. All that you require is an internet connection and a modern web browser.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How can I log in to my account?">
All you need to do is type your account URL into the web browser and enter your login credentials.

To log in:
- Enter your company URL
- Use your registered email address
- Enter your password

If login fails, contact support@proofhub.com
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What browsers does ProofHub support?">
We support the latest stable versions of:

- Chrome
- Safari
- Firefox
- Edge
- Chromium
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Do I get limited features in the trial period?">
No, you get access to all the features of ProofHub during the trial.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens when the trial period ends?">
If the trial period ends before you choose a plan, your account will freeze.

Your data remains intact but inaccessible until you subscribe to a plan and add payment details.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="How do I get started with ProofHub?">
Getting started with ProofHub is easy:

1. Add people
2. Add projects
3. Personalize your workspace

You can also follow the getting started guide.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I personalize my account?">
Yes, you can white-label your account by:
- Adding a company logo
- Creating a custom URL
- Selecting theme colors
- Setting account themes
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I import people from a CSV file?">
Yes, you can import users using a CSV file in ProofHub.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can recurring tasks be created?">
Yes. You need to set the due date first before configuring recurrence.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What payment methods do you accept?">
We accept:
- PayPal
- Visa
- Mastercard
- American Express
- Bank wire transfers (annual subscriptions)
</Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
