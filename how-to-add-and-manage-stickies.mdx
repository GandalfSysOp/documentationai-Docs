---
title: How to add and manage stickies
---
Stickies in ProofHub provide a convenient way to jot down notes, reminders, and important information, keeping your workspace organized and clutter-free. This guide will walk you through the steps to add, manage, archive, delete, and change the color of stickies and how to unarchive them. Stickies are unique to the individual creating them and are not shared with other collaborators.

## **Add a sticky**

1. **Access stickies**:

   - Go to the right-side menu and click on the “**Stickies**” option.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-12.38.27-PM.png" width="516" height="756" alt="" />

2. **Add a sticky**:\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-12.41.08-PM.png" width="445" height="753" alt="" />

   - Click on the “**Add**” button.

   - Enter a title and description for your sticky.

   - Click on the three dots to open the color options.

   - Select a color for your sticky.

   - Click “**Done**” to save your sticky.

---

## **Managing stickies**

1. **Edit a sticky**:\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-12.44.25-PM.png" width="424" height="752" alt="" />

   - Click on an existing sticky to start editing it.

   - Make the necessary changes to the title or description.

   - Click “**Done**” to save your sticky.

---

## **Archive stickies**

1. Click on the three dots next to an existing sticky.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-12.45.22-PM.png" width="421" height="752" alt="" />

2. Select “**Archive**” from the dropdown menu.

---

## **Unarchive stickies**

1. **Access archived stickies**:

   - Right next to the stickies search option, click on the three dots.

   - Select “**Archived**” from the dropdown menu.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-12.47.10-PM.png" width="437" height="753" alt="" />

2. **Unarchive a sticky**:

   - Click on the three dots next to the archived sticky.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-12.51.21-PM.png" width="412" height="753" alt="" />

   - Select “**Unarchive**” from the dropdown menu.

---

## **Delete stickies**

1. Click on the three dots next to an existing sticky.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-12.52.58-PM.png" width="407" height="752" alt="" />

2. Select “**Delete**“.

---

## **Change stickies color**

1. Click on the three dots next to an existing sticky.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-12.54.54-PM.png" width="410" height="754" alt="" />
