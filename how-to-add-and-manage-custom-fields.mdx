## **Introduction to custom fields**

ProofHub takes project management a step further with custom fields. Capture the specific information that matters most to your projects. Go beyond basic task details and add fields for things like budgets, deadlines, priorities, or anything unique to your workflow. This guide will show you how to create custom fields, choose the right data type (text, numbers, dates, etc.), and leverage them to gain deeper insights into your projects.

## **Types of custom fields**

1. **Text:**

   - **Description:** The text field allows you to save data of any type in a single line.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.30.21-AM.png" width="1092" height="754" alt="" />

   - **Use case:** Ideal for capturing short, concise information such as names, titles, or keywords.

2. **Textarea:**

   - **Description:** The textarea field is similar to the text field but allows data entry that appears in multiple lines.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.32.56-AM.png" width="1093" height="754" alt="" />

   - **Use case:** Suitable for longer descriptions, notes, or comments requiring more space.

3. **Dropdown:**

   - **Description:** The dropdown field enables you to create a list of selectable options. Customize the options and apply color coding for better visual distinction.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.28.16-AM.png" width="1095" height="754" alt="" />

   - **Use case:** Great for categorizing tasks or selecting specific attributes from a predefined list.

4. **Multi-select:**

   - **Description:** The multi-select field allows you to choose multiple customized values.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.47.34-AM.png" width="1096" height="753" alt="" />

   - **Use case:** Useful when a task or item can belong to more than one category or attribute.

5. **Numbers:**

   - **Description:** The numbers field is used to store numeric data, including negative, positive, decimal, and exponential figures.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.49.58-AM.png" width="1097" height="754" alt="" />

   - **Use case:** Ideal for tracking quantities, amounts, or numerical metrics associated with tasks.

6. **Percentage:**

   - **Description:** The percentage field is designed to store percentage data.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.51.25-AM.png" width="1094" height="753" alt="" />

   - **Use case:** Useful for tracking progress, completion rates, or any other metric represented as a percentage.

7. **Currency:**

   - **Description:** The currency field stores data in different currency types. ProofHub supports default currency types such as $ USD, $ CAD, £ GBP, € EUR, and more. Additional currency types can be added.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.52.56-AM.png" width="1090" height="751" alt="" />

   - **Use case:** Essential for managing financial data and costs associated with tasks.

8. **Date:**

   - **Description:** The date field allows you to save specific dates related to tasks.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.54.41-AM.png" width="1092" height="753" alt="" />

   - **Use case:** Helpful for setting task deadlines, milestones, or tracking time-sensitive information.

9. **Priority:**

   - **Description:** The priority field helps define task priority levels within a task list. It can be added once per task list and comes with predefined priority stages.


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.55.58-AM.png" width="1094" height="752" alt="" />



     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-12.00.21-PM.png" width="1147" height="718" alt="" />

   - **Use case:** This enables you to categorize and prioritize tasks based on urgency or importance.

## **Add custom fields**

**<u>Method 1:</u>****&#x20;Add a new custom field from the table view**

1. **<u>Open the custom field options:</u>** Start by accessing the “**Table view**” within the tasks section of your project in ProofHub. Scroll to the far right of the screen where you’ll find a plus icon (**+**).


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-10.09.49-AM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

2. **<u>Custom field type:</u>** Click on the plus icon, then choose your desired custom field type from the “**Add field**” option.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-10.15.03-AM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

3. **<u>Configure custom field details:</u>** After selecting the field type, provide the necessary details based on the type chosen:


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-10.22.12-AM.png" width="715" height="467" alt="" />

   - **Field name/title:** Enter a name for the custom field.

   - **Field description:** Optionally, add a description for the field. This description will appear as a tooltip when you hover over the field name.

   - **Field configuration:** Depending on the selected field type, make any required changes or configurations (e.g., defining dropdown options, setting default values, etc.).

4. **<u>Save the custom field:</u>** Click on the ‘**Add**‘ button to save the field.

**<u>Method 2:</u>****&#x20;Add a new custom field from the task window**

1. **<u>Open the custom fields section:</u>** Open the task window of the specific task where you want to add a custom field. Within the task window, click on the “**Add field**” option.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-10.24.51-AM.png" width="1148" height="713" alt="" />

2. **<u>Configure custom field details:</u>** After selecting the field type, provide the necessary details based on the type chosen:


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-10.22.12-AM.png" width="715" height="467" alt="" />

   - **Field name/title:** Enter a name for the custom field.

   - **Field description:** Optionally, add a description for the field. This description will appear as a tooltip when you hover over the field name.

   - **Field configuration:** Depending on the selected field type, make any required changes or configurations (e.g., defining dropdown options, setting default values, etc.).

3. **<u>Save the custom field:</u>** Click on the ‘**Add**‘ button to save the field.

## **Edit custom fields**

**<u>Method 1:</u>****&#x20;Edit a custom field from the table view**

1. **<u>Locate custom field:</u>** In the Table view, locate the custom field you wish to edit. Look for the field name displayed as a column header. Click on the arrow icon to reveal a dropdown menu. Choose the ‘**Edit field**‘ option.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-10.42.40-AM.png" width="1440" height="753" alt="" />

2. **<u>Modify field details:</u>** Within the edit field window, you can update the field title and description as needed, depending on the custom field type.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-10.46.45-AM.png" width="714" height="595" alt="" />

3. **<u>Save changes:</u>** Note that the field type of a custom field cannot be edited. After making the desired changes, click the ‘**Update**‘ option to save the updated field details.

**<u>Method 2:</u>****&#x20;Edit a custom field from the task window**

1. **<u>Open task details:</u>** Click on the specific task where the custom field is located to open the task window. Within the task window, select ‘**Manage fields**‘.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-10.53.23-AM.png" width="1146" height="717" alt="" />

2. **<u>Open edit options:</u>** Click on the three dots when you hover to the right and select ‘**Edit**‘.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-10.57.59-AM.png" width="820" height="219" alt="" />

3. **<u>Update field details:</u>** Easily edit the field title and description within the edit field window depending on the custom field type.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.01.52-AM.png" width="716" height="576" alt="" />

4. **<u>Save changes:</u>** Click on the ‘**Update**‘ option to save the changes to the custom field.

## **Delete custom fields**

**<u>Method 1:</u>****&#x20;Delete a custom field from the table view**

1. **<u>Locate custom field:</u>** In the Table view, locate the custom field you wish to delete. Look for the field name displayed as a column header. Click on the arrow icon to reveal a dropdown menu. Select the ‘**Delete field**‘.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.11.38-AM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

2. **<u>Confirmation prompt:</u>** Select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to proceed with the deletion.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.13.57-AM.png" width="384" height="172" alt="" />

**<u>Note:</u>** *ProofHub keeps items in the “Trash” for 15 days. Restore them easily by going to Trash. For detailed instructions on restoring deleted items from the trash, please refer to&#xA0;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/manage/how-to-manage-trash" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*Trash*</a>*.*

**<u>Method 2:</u>****&#x20;Delete a custom field from the task window**

1. **<u>Open task details:</u>** Click on the specific task where the custom field is located to open the task window. Within the task window, select ‘**Manage fields**‘.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-10.53.23-AM.png" width="1146" height="717" alt="" />

2. **<u>Open edit options:</u>** Click on the three dots when you hover to the right and select ‘**Delete**‘.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.15.24-AM.png" width="815" height="221" alt="" />

3. **<u>Confirmation prompt:</u>** Select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to proceed with the deletion.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-11.13.57-AM.png" width="384" height="172" alt="" />

**<u>Note:</u>** *Custom fields will not be accessible outside the specific task list in which they were created.* *ProofHub keeps items in the “Trash” for 15 days. Restore them easily by going to Trash. For detailed instructions on restoring deleted items from the trash, please refer to&#xA0;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/manage/how-to-manage-trash" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*Trash*</a>*.*
