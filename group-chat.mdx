---
title: Group chat
---
ProofHub’s group chat feature enables you to create groups for effortless discussions with everyone involved in a project or task. Instantly exchange messages, pictures, and files with everyone involved, keeping communication flowing and everyone on the same page.

## **Create a group**

1. **<u>Navigate the chat window:</u>** Select **“+”** icon at the top left side of the window.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/create-group-1.png" width="467" height="788" alt="" />

2. Select the create group option.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/create-group-2.png" width="472" height="783" alt="" />

3. **<u>Build your group:</u>** In the create group window you can:\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/create-group-3.png" width="703" height="305" alt="" />

   - **Craft a group name:** Give your group chat a clear and descriptive title to reflect its purpose.

   - **Add team members:** Select the individuals you want to include in the group chat by searching for their names or browsing the team list.

4. **<u>Finalize the group:</u>** Once you’ve added all desired participants, click the **“Add”** button to create your group chat.\


---

## **Leave group**

1. **<u>Select the group:</u>** In the group chat window click the three dots and select the **“Leave group”** option.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/chat-leave-group.png" width="457" height="549" alt="" />

2. Select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to leave the group.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/confirm-leave-group.png" width="437" height="169" alt="" />

---

## **Manage group**

1. In the group chat window click the three dots and select the “**Manage group”&#xA0;**&#x6F;ption\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Manage-group-1.png" width="457" height="549" alt="" />

2. Update the group details like the name of the group or manage the people in the group and select **“Update”** to confirm the changes.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Manage-group-2.png" width="697" height="310" alt="" />



---

## **Delete group**

1. In the group chat window click the three dots and select the “**Delete group”&#xA0;**&#x6F;ption.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/delete-chat-group-1.png" width="457" height="549" alt="" />

2. Select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to delete the group.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/confirm-deletion-group.png" width="433" height="167" alt="" />

**<u>Note:</u>****&#xA0;***Once a group is deleted, it cannot be restored.*
