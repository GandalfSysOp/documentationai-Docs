---
title: Introduction to tasks
---
ProofHub helps you stay on top of your projects with its task management features. Here’s a quick rundown:

- **Tasklists:** ProofHub task lists are the backbone of keeping your projects organized. They let you break down your work into actionable steps, making it easy to assign tasks, set deadlines, and track progress – all within a centralized location. You can create public or private task lists, associate them with workflows, and even track time spent on them.

- **Tasks:** Within each task list, you can add individual tasks. Each task can have a title, description, due date, assignees, labels, and even the estimated time required. ProofHub allows you to attach files and collaborate on tasks directly.

Together, ProofHub tasks and task lists empower teams to stay organized, focused, and productive by providing a centralized platform for managing tasks and collaborating efficiently toward project success.
