---
title: Login
---
To log in, you can either enter your company URL in the address bar of your browser or you can go to <a href="https://www.proofhub.com/login" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https\://www\.proofhub.com/login</a> and enter your company URL. After this, you need to follow the below-mentioned steps:

1. Enter your email address and click on **Next.**\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/login-1.png" width="496" height="569" alt="Login in ProofHub" />

2. Enter your password and click on **Log in**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/08/login-1-1.png" width="482" height="604" alt="Add Password in ProofHub" />

   \
   \
   **Note:**\
   \
   *To log in with a different email ID, click on “Back to login,” and you will be redirected to the screen where you initially entered your email ID.*
