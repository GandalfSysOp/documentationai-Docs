---
title: How to use email-in in discussions
---
ProofHub’s email-in feature allows you to post comments while you are on the go, without the need to access your account. Each topic of discussion in ProofHub has a unique email address that can be saved and used later for posting comments on discussion topics.

## **Comment via email-in**

1. Click the three dots or right-click on the topic.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/comment-via-Email-in.png" width="611" height="493" alt="ProofHub's comment via Email-in" />



2. Select **“Copy email-in address”** and the email address will be copied to the clipboard.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/30.png" width="1381" height="497" alt="" />



3. **&#xA0;**&#x43;ompose an email using your emailing service and paste the email-in address in the recipient field and type your comment in the body of the mail. Clic&#x6B;**&#xA0;“Send”** and your comment will be added to the discussion topic.

## **Reply via email-in**

ProofHub keeps you notified with emails whenever someone adds a comment to a discussion. You can simply reply directly to that notification email to add your own comment to the conversation – no need to log in.

**<u>Note:</u>** *For private discussions, only assigned members have the ability to comment and reply via email-in. This ensures discussions with sensitive information remain secure.*
