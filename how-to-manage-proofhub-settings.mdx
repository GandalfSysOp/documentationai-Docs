## **Custom domain**

Setting up a custom domain in ProofHub allows you to personalize your ProofHub URL to align with your brand identity.

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Proofhub settings**“ section and select “**Custom domain**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-5.07.11-PM.png" width="596" height="753" alt="" />

2. **<u>Set custom domain:</u>** In the custom domain section, enter your desired custom domain or sub-domain (e.g., <a href="http://hub.yoursite.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">hub.yoursite.com</a> or <a href="http://projects.yoursite.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">projects.yoursite.com</a>).\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-4.13.31-PM.png" width="1440" height="753" alt="" />

3. Click on “**Update**” to save changes.\
   \
   **CNAME :&#xA0;**&#x54;o point your domain through CNAME, you can perform the following steps:

4. Login to your domain host.

5. Put the hostname as the URL you want to use to access your ProofHub account.

6. Point the CNAME record to: <a href="http://customdomain.proofhub.com" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">**customdomain.proofhub.com**</a>

7. You’re done with redirecting your domain to ProofHub server through CNAME.

**<u>Note:</u>** *Omit “*<a href="http://”" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*http\://”*</a>*&#x20;or “*<a href="https://”" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*https\://”*</a>*, and do not include a folder path like “hub.yoursite.com/folder”. Allow 24-48 hours for the DNS changes to propagate fully. During this period, your custom domain may not be immediately accessible.*

## **Preferences**

Managing your company’s preferences in ProofHub allows you to customize various settings to better align with your organizational policies and workflows. These adjustments apply to the entire company, ensuring consistency across all projects and users.

**Change date format, time format, and start week**

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Proofhub settings**” section and select “**Preferences**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-4.25.05-PM.png" width="597" height="753" alt="" />

   \


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-4.17.41-PM.png" width="1440" height="877" alt="" />

   - **Date format:** From the dropdown menu, select your preferred date format (e.g., MM/DD/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY).

   - **Time format:** From the dropdown menu, select your preferred time format (e.g., 12-hour, 24-hour).

   - **Start week from:** From the dropdown menu, select the day you want the week to start (e.g., Sunday, Monday).

   - **Save changes:** Click the “**Update**” button to apply your new preferences.

---

**Working days**

1. Check or uncheck the days of the week to reflect your organization’s working days.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-4.29.35-PM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

   \
   **<u>Note:</u>** *Updating working days does not affect existing tasks, events, milestones, and projects.*

2. **<u>Save changes:</u>** Click the “**Update**” button to apply your new preferences.

---

**Email notifications**

1. Check or uncheck to turn on/off all email notifications for everyone in the entire account.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-4.33.17-PM.png" width="1440" height="749" alt="" />

2. **<u>Save changes:</u>** Click the “**Update**” button to apply your new preferences.

---

**Password policy**

1. Check the box to allow users to only set strong passwords for their accounts.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-10.05.20-AM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

2. **<u>Save changes:</u>** Click the “**Update**” button to apply your new preferences.
