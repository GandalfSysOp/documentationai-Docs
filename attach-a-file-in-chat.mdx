---
title: Attach a file in chat
---
ProofHub chat allows you to share photos, screenshots, and files with your text messages, enabling instant feedback and approvals from team members.

1. In the chat window look for the **“+”** icon at the bottom. Click on the paperclip icon to open the file attachment menu.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/attach-file-1.png" width="372" height="487" alt="" />

2. **<u>Select Your File:</u>** A window will appear allowing you to browse your local device storage. Navigate to the file you want to share and select it.

3. **<u>Send and Collaborate:</u>**<u> </u>Once you’ve chosen the file, hit **“Enter”** on your keyboard. Your file will be uploaded and posted within your chat conversation.

- Note:

  - *You can see files posted earlier by scrolling up through the chat history.*

  - *To download a file from chat, right-click on the file and select&#xA0;****Save as****.&#xA0;*
