---
title: How to log time in a task
---
Logging your time in ProofHub helps keep track of the effort spent on tasks. Follow these steps to accurately log your time:

## **Add time using the timer**

1. **<u>Open the task</u>**: Select the specific task you want to log time for.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-10.38.26-AM.png" width="1144" height="715" alt="" />


   - In the task details, find the **“Time”** field. Click on the **“Add logged time”** field.

   - You will see two options:

     - **Use timer**

     - **Track manually**

2. **<u>Using the timer:</u>** If you select **“Use timer”**, the timer will start running within the task. The running timer will be visible at the top of the task window.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-10.40.51-AM.png" width="1150" height="715" alt="" />

3. **<u>Managing the timer:</u>** To stop the timer, click on the timer arrow.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-10.42.15-AM.png" width="1147" height="714" alt="" />

   - You will have two options:

     - **“Save time”**

     - **“Discard”**

4. **<u>Saving the timer:</u>** Select **“Save time”** to open the time logging window.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-10.44.16-AM.png" width="713" height="714" alt="" />

   - In the time logging window:

     - **Select the timesheet** where you want to log the time.

     - **Change the date, hour, and minutes** if needed.

     - **Change the status** (e.g., Billed, Billable, Void) as necessary.

5. Click the **“Add”** button to save the logged time.

---

## **Add time manually**

1. **<u>Open the task</u>**: Select the specific task you want to log time for. This will open the task details.

   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-10.38.26-AM.png" width="1144" height="715" alt="" />

   - In the task details, find the **“Time”** field. Click on the **“Add logged time”** field.

   - You will see two options:

     - **Use timer**

     - **Track manually**

2. **<u>Using “Track manually”:</u>** If you select **“Track manually”**, the time logging window will open directly.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-10.44.16-AM.png" width="713" height="714" alt="" />

   - In the time logging window:

     - **Select the timesheet** where you want to log the time.

     - **Change the date** if needed.

     - **Manually enter the hours and minutes** spent on the task.

     - **Change the status** (e.g., Billed, Billable, Void) as necessary.

3. Click the **“Add”** button to save the manually logged time.

---

## **Add estimated time in a task**

1. **<u>Estimated time option:</u>** In the task details, find the **“Time”** field. Click on the **“Add estimated time”** option.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-11.01.00-AM.png" width="1150" height="716" alt="" />

2. **<u>Enter estimated time:</u>**


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-11.02.59-AM.png" width="1147" height="714" alt="" />

   - Enter **“Hours”** and **“Minutes”** to input the estimated time required for the task.

   - Click the **“Update”** button to set the estimated time.

3. **<u>View the estimated time:</u>** The estimated time will be visible in the task window under the “**Time**” field.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-11.04.23-AM.png" width="1145" height="715" alt="" />

**<u>Note:</u>** *An email notification will be sent if the logged time exceeds the estimated time.*
