---
title: How to utilize all gantt
---
All gantt in ProofHub offers a clear and detailed overview of task advancement across multiple projects, bringing transparency and clarity to your workflows.

1. Click on “**Everything**” and select **“All gantt”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/all-gantt-1.png" width="428" height="424" alt="" />

2. In the “**All gantt**” view, you will see a list of all tasks on gantt from all your projects.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/all-gantt-2.png" width="1322" height="437" alt="" />

## **Filters**

Click on the filter icon at the top-right corner of the page.\


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/all-gantt-3.png" width="1325" height="313" alt="" />

**Projects:** You can select one or multiple projects to display in the gantt view and click **“Apply”**.

**Assigned:** You can select a specific person or all team members to view their tasks in the gantt view click **“Apply”**.

## **Export**

There are two ways you can export the gantt in ProofHub.\


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/all-gantt-4.png" width="1367" height="401" alt="" />

1. Click on the three dots icon at the top-right of the page.

2. Select “**Print”&#xA0;**&#x74;o print the gantt chart.

3. Or else, download the gantt chart in PDF format by choosing the “**Export as PDF**” option. 

**<u>Note:</u>****&#xA0;***If you can’t fit the gantt chart on* one screen while exporting/printing, then try changing the settings as follow&#x73;*:*

- *Change the DPI to “****200***“

- *Select the orientation to “****Landscape***“

- Select the paper format to “**A3**“
