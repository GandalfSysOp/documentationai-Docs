---
title: How to access dark mode
---
ProofHub allows you to customize the appearance of the interface by switching between light mode, and dark mode or automatically adapting to your system settings.

1. **<u>Access mode settings</u>**<u>:</u>

   - Click on your profile icon located at the bottom left corner of the ProofHub interface.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-07-01-at-3.52.36-PM.png" width="468" height="569" alt="Dark Mode in ProofHub" />

2. **<u>Select mode option</u>**: Under the “**Mode**” option, you will see three choices:

   - **Light**: Select this option to switch to light mode.

   - **Dark**: Select this option to switch to dark mode.

   - **System**: Select this option to automatically adapt to your system settings.

3. **<u>Apply the mode</u>**<u>:</u>

   - Your selected mode will be applied immediately, changing the appearance of the ProofHub interface accordingly.
