---
## **Comments on note**

1. **<u>Write your comment, add emojis, or attach files & documents</u>**<u>:</u> Type your feedback or message in the comment box. Use the emoji icon to insert emojis into your comment. Click the attachment icon to upload and attach files.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-14-at-12.18.39-PM.png" width="1218" height="715" alt="" />

2. **<u>Click add:</u>** After composing your comment, click the ‘**Add**‘ button to post it.

---

## **Comment approvals**

1. **<u>Resolve a comment</u>**<u>:</u> Identify the comment you want to approve or acknowledge. Click on the “**Resolve**” option within that comment.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/3-3.png" width="1221" height="715" alt="" />

2. **<u>Confirmation of approval</u>**<u>:</u>\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/3-4.png" width="1220" height="715" alt="" />

   - Once marked as resolved, it will be highlighted in green.

   - Hover over the “**Reopen**” option to view the name of the person who resolved it and the timestamp of when it was resolved.

   - If you later want to reconsider or reopen the resolved comment, click on the “**Reopen**” option that replaces the “**Resolve**” button.

   - This action will revert the resolved status, allowing further discussion or changes.

**<u>Note:</u>** *By following these steps, you can effectively manage comments within ProofHub notes by approving suggestions or feedback using the “Resolve” feature and reopening them if necessary.*

---

## **@Mention in notes comment**

1. Type “@” within the comment box. This will trigger a list of subscribers associated with the note.

2. **<u>Select subscriber</u>**<u>:</u> From the list that appears, select the subscriber you want to mention. This will tag them in your comment.\
