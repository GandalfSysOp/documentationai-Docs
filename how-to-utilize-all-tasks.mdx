title: How to utilize all tasks
---
The “**All tasks**” view in ProofHub provides a comprehensive overview of all tasks across projects, helping you stay organized and up-to-date.

## **All tasks**

1. Click on “**Everything**” and select the **“All tasks”** option.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-3.31.20-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="" />

2. In the “**All tasks**” view, you will see a list of all tasks from all your projects.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.52.31-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="All tasks in ProofHub" />

---

## **Different views**

1. **<u>Board view:</u>**


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-9.08.51-PM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="All tasks - Board view in ProofHub" />

   - **Select a workflow**: When you switch to board view, you must select a specific workflow.

   - **View tasks**: After selecting the workflow, the board will display all tasks from different projects that follow the chosen workflow.

2. **<u>Table view:</u>**


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.53.35-PM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="All tasks - Table view in ProofHub" />

   - View tasks from all projects in one place.

   - Sort and manage tasks using different columns.

---

## **Filters**

To refine your task list, you can apply various filters:


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.55.38-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="All tasks filter in ProofHub" />

1. **<u>Locate filters</u>**<u>:</u> Find the filter option on the top right side of the “**All tasks**” page.

2. **<u>Set filters</u>**<u>:</u> Click on the filter icon, choose your desired criteria (such as due date, assignee, status, etc.), and click “**Apply**” to see the filtered tasks.

---

## **Export**

Exporting your tasks is straightforward with ProofHub. Click on the three dots next to the filter option to access the export option:


<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-06-24-at-5.58.07-PM.png" width="1440" height="753" alt="All tasks export in ProofHub" />

1. **<u>Download as CSV</u>**<u>:</u> This option lets you download the entire list of tasks in CSV format, which can be useful for offline analysis or integration with other tools.

2. **<u>Export in email</u>**<u>:</u> Choose this to have the task export file emailed to you, allowing you to share it easily with others or keep a backup.

3. **<u>Show columns</u>**<u>:</u> Customize the visibility of columns in your task list. This option allows you to hide columns that are not relevant to you, helping to declutter the view.

**<u>Note:</u>** *Custom fields will not be accessible here.*
