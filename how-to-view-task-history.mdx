---
title: How to view task history
---
The task history feature in ProofHub allows you to track all activities and changes associated with a task from its creation onwards.

Follow these steps to access and view the task history:

1. **<u>Navigate to task history:</u>** Click on any task to open the task window. Inside the task window, look for the panel on the right side. Select the “**Task history**” tab.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-1.37.21-PM.png" width="1147" height="714" alt="" />

2. **<u>View task history:</u>** Once you click on the “**Task history**” tab, you will see a chronological list of all activities performed on the task since its creation. This includes actions such as task updates, status changes, and more.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-1.39.11-PM.png" width="1148" height="715" alt="" />

3. **<u>Task activity details:</u>** Click on any entry in the task history log. This will expand the activity to display further details, providing insights into who made the change, when it occurred, and what modifications were made.


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-16-at-1.40.31-PM.png" width="1144" height="714" alt="" />
