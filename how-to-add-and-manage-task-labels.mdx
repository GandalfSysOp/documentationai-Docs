---
title: How to add and manage task labels
---
ProofHub’s task labels are a way to categorize and prioritize your tasks. They act like tags that you can add to tasks to help you:

- **<u>Organize:</u>** Group similar tasks together based on urgency, stage of completion, or any other criteria you define using labels.

- **<u>Prioritize:</u>** Easily identify important tasks by assigning labels like “High Priority” or “Urgent.”

- **<u>Filter:</u>** Quickly find specific tasks by filtering based on assigned labels.

These labels are completely customizable, allowing you to create a system that works for your specific needs and workflow.

## **Add a new task label**

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Manage”** section and select “**Task labels**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.13.22-PM.png" width="594" height="754" alt="" />

2. **<u>Add a new task label:</u>**\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.16.51-PM.png" width="1440" height="753" alt="" />

   - Click on the **“Add”** button.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.19.29-PM.png" width="717" height="337" alt="" />

   - Enter a title for your new task label.

   - Projects – Select “**All**” to allow labels to be used in all projects or just for &#x61;**&#x20;**&#x66;ew projects.

   - Click **“Add”**.

---

## **Edit a task label**

1. Right-click or click on the three dots and select “**Edit**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.25.50-PM.png" width="1038" height="263" alt="" />

2. From the edit window make changes as required.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.28.50-PM.png" width="718" height="336" alt="" />

   - Change the task title or task label as required.

   - Click “**Update**” to save changes.

---

## **Delete a task label**

1. Right-click or click on the three dots and select **“Delete**”.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.35.06-PM.png" width="1041" height="259" alt="" />

2. Select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to proceed with deleting the task label.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.39.58-PM.png" width="447" height="175" alt="" />
