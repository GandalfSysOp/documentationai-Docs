## **Comments on discussions**

1. Click on the topic you want to comment on.

2. **<u>Write your comment, add emojis, or attach files & documents</u>**: Type your feedback or message in the comment box. Use the emoji icon to insert emojis into your comment. Click the attachment icon to upload and attach files.

3. **<u>@Mentions</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x59;ou can mention other people in your comments using th&#x65;**&#xA0;‘@’** symbol followed by the name of the person. Email notifications will be sent out to the mentioned person.

4. After composing your comment, click the **‘Add’** button to post it.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/44.png" width="944" height="774" alt="" />



## **Proof & approve topic files**

1. **&#xA0;**&#x4E;avigate to the topic containing the file you wish to proof & approve.

2.  Find the file within the topic and select **“Proof this file”** to access the proofing window.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/45.png" width="933" height="788" alt="" />



3. **<u>Approval process</u>****:** Within the proofing window, you can approve the file or utilize markup tools such as rectangular boxes or arrows to highlight areas for improvement in the file or image.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/46.png" width="1421" height="770" alt="" />
