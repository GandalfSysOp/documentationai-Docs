---
title: How to change your password
---
1. **<u>Access change password settings</u>**<u>:</u> Click on your profile icon located at the bottom left corner of the ProofHub interface and select “**Change password**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-07-01-at-3.47.48-PM.png" width="401" height="569" alt="Change Password in ProofHub" />

2. **<u>Enter password</u>**:\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-11.41.24-AM.png" width="716" height="392" alt="" />

   - Enter your current password.

   - Enter your new password in the “**New password**” field.

   - Confirm your new password by entering it again in the “**Confirm password**” field.

3. **<u>Logout from all devices</u>**:

   - If you want to log out from all devices, check the option “**Logout from all devices**“.

4. **<u>Save new password</u>**:

   - Click the “**Update**” button to change your password.
