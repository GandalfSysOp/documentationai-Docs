---
title: Introduction to notes
---
Notes is a versatile feature within the ProofHub platform that expands beyond traditional project management functions. It offers a flexible tool for creating, organizing, and sharing information effectively in the form of notes, serving as a dynamic knowledge base or wiki.

Users can leverage Notes to compile and share valuable insights, procedures, and reference materials across different teams and projects. With its robust capabilities to format text, embed media, and collaborate in real-time, Notes ensures that information remains organized and readily accessible, fostering a collaborative and knowledgeable environment.

This feature provides a dedicated space to store creative ideas, thoughts, and critical project details, allowing quick access whenever needed.

Here’s what you can achieve:

1. **Build a knowledge base:** Capture workflows, outline best practices, and compile common challenges and solutions to find answers and get up to speed. This keeps everyone on the same page and ensures consistent, high-quality work.

2. **Streamline project planning:** Outline tasks, timelines, and responsibilities using notes. Keep all project details centralized for easy access by everyone involved.

3. **Organize research and information:** Compile research findings, data, and important links within notes. This simplifies resource gathering and streamlines presentations.

4. **Plan & collaborate on content creation:** Use notes to plan and outline content like articles, blogs, or marketing materials. Team members can collaborate and refine content before publishing.
