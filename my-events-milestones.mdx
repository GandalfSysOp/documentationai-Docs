---
title: My events & milestones
---
ProofHub’s “My events & milestones” option centralizes all your scheduled activities and key project milestones in one convenient location. Whether you’re managing multiple projects or collaborating across different teams, this feature allows you to view and track your personal events and important deadlines from all projects you are subscribed to. It helps you stay organized, ensures you never miss a crucial date, and provides a comprehensive overview of your commitments and progress, all within a single, easy-to-navigate interface.\
