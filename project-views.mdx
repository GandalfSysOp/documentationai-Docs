title: Project Views
---
In the project section, you can view all projects, those specifically assigned to you, and access templates, all conveniently in one place.

<Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/View-1.png" width="450" height="509" alt="" />

1. **<u>Assigned to me:</u>&#xA0;**&#x41;ll the projects that have been assigned to you.

   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/assigned-to-me.png" width="1371" height="448" alt="" />

   \
   Use the sort and filter options to quickly locate, organize, and access projects based on specific criteria, enhancing productivity and workflow management. 

   - **Status:** Filter projects by status.

   - **Category:** Filter projects by different project categories.

   - **Colors:** Filter projects according to the colors assigned to them.

   - **Sort by:** You can sort by category, latest updates, name, and status.

2. **<u>Managed by me:</u>&#xA0;**&#x41;ll the projects where you have been assigned as a project manager.

   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Managed-by-me.png" width="1367" height="398" alt="" />

   \
   Use the sort and filter options to quickly locate, organize, and access projects based on specific criteria, enhancing productivity and workflow management. 

   - **Status:** Filter projects by status.

   - **Category:** Filter projects by different project categories.

   - **Colors:** Filter projects according to the colors assigned to them.

   - **Sort by:** You can sort by category, latest updates, name, and status.

3. **<u>All projects:</u>&#xA0;**&#x41;ll the projects across the account.

   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/All-projects.png" width="1362" height="434" alt="" />

   \
   Use the sort and filter options to quickly locate, organize, and access all projects based on specific criteria, enhancing productivity and workflow management. 

   - **Status:** Filter projects by status.

   - **Category:** Filter projects by different project categories.

   - **Assigned:** Filter projects by the person they are assigned to or show all projects.

   - **Colors:** Filter projects according to the colors assigned to them.

   - **Sort by:** You can sort by category, latest updates, name, and status.

4. **<u>Templates:</u>&#xA0;**&#x41;ll the templates created across the account. For more on templates, <a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/projects/what-is-a-template" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">click here</a>.

   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Templates.png" width="1371" height="339" alt="" />

   \
   Use the sort and filter options to quickly locate, organize, and access templates based on specific criteria, enhancing productivity and workflow management. 

   - **Colors:** Filter templates according to the colors assigned to them.

   - **Assigned:** Filter templates by the person they are assigned to or show all templates.
