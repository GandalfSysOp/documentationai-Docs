## **Add a topic**

1. **<u>Navigate to the discussion section</u>****:** Select the specific project where you want to create the topic. Click the **“Discussions”** tab on the left-side menu.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/41.png" width="337" height="464" alt="" />

   \


2. **<u>Add a new topic</u>****:** Click on the **“+”** icon to initiate the creation of a new topic.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/42.png" width="342" height="492" alt="" />



3. **<u>Enter topic details</u>****:**\
   \


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/43.png" width="696" height="406" alt="" />

   \


   - **Title and description:** Provide a title and description for your topic.

   - **Subscribers:** To notify specific team members about this topic, select them from the subscribers list.

   - **Mark as private:&#xA0;**&#x43;heck the “**Mark as private**” option if you need to restrict the visibility of the topic to selected subscribers only.

   - **Attach:&#xA0;**&#x42;y attaching files, you can streamline communication, provide context, and ensure everyone has the information they need to contribute effectively.

   - **Pin to top:&#x20;**&#x54;his option lets you prioritize important conversations by placing them at the top of the discussion list.

4. **<u>Save the topic</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x43;lick the **“Add”** button to save your new topic.

---

## **Manage people on the topic**

1. Click the three dots or right-click on the topic and select **“Manage people”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Manage-people.png" width="569" height="454" alt="" />

2. **<u>Manage subscribers</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x54;he list of existing subscribers will be available. To add or remove subscribers from the topic select **“Manage people”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Manage-people-2-1.png" width="674" height="445" alt="" />



3. **<u>Update subscribers</u>**: Tick the checkbox next to the names of the people you want to add as subscribers or uncheck to remove them from subscribers and click on the “**Update**” button to save and apply the changes.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/40.png" width="679" height="648" alt="" />

---

## **Edit a topic**

1. Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the topic and select “**Edit**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/manage-people-edit.png" width="567" height="424" alt="" />

2. **<u>Edit the details</u>****:&#xA0;**&#xA0;Modify the topic details you would like to edit and select **“Update”** to confirm the changes.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/34.png" width="705" height="412" alt="" />

   \
   **<u>Note:</u>***&#xA0;If you edit an archived topic, it will become an active topic of discussion.*

## **Delete a topic**

1. **&#xA0;**&#x43;lick on the three dots icon or right-click on the topic and select “**Delete**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/manage-people-delete.png" width="567" height="424" alt="" />

2. **<u>Confirm deletion</u>****:&#xA0;**&#x53;elect “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to proceed with deleting the topic.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/36.png" width="437" height="202" alt="" />

**<u>Note: </u>***ProofHub keeps items in the “Trash” for 15 days. Restore them easily by going to Trash. For detailed instructions on restoring deleted items from the trash, please refer to&#xA0;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/manage/how-to-manage-trash" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*Trash*</a>*.*

   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Unarchive-topic.png" width="599" height="340" alt="" />

2. **<u>Select the topic:</u>****&#xA0;**&#x4F;n the archived topic, click the three dots, or right-click to select **“Unarchive”**.\

## **Convert existing topic to private**

1. Find the topic and click the three dots or right-click.

2. **<u>Edit</u>****:** Choose **“Edit”** from the context menu to modify the discussion.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/manage-people-edit-1.png" width="567" height="424" alt="" />

3. **<u>Enable privacy</u>****:** Check the **“Mark as private”** option to restrict participation.

4. **<u>Save the changes</u>****:** Clic&#x6B;**&#x20;“Update”** to confirm the changes and apply the privacy settings to the discussion.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/existing-private-topic2.png" width="700" height="410" alt="" />
