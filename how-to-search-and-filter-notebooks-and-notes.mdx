---
title: How to search and filter notebooks and notes
---
## **Search notebooks**

1. **<u>Open the notes section</u>**<u>:</u> Navigate to the project and click on the “**Notes**” section.

2. **<u>Search for a notebook</u>**<u>:</u>\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-5.28.05-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="" />

   - Click on the search icon to activate the search bar.

   - Start typing the name of the notebook you are looking for in the search bar.

   - As you type, ProofHub will display a list of notebooks that match your search query.

## **Search notes**

1. **<u>Search for a note</u>**<u>:</u> Click on the notebook that contains the note you want to search for.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-5.08.07-PM.png" width="1436" height="753" alt="" />

   - In the top right corner of the notebook view, locate the search bar.

   - Type the name of the note you want to find into the search bar.

---

## **Filter notes**

1. **<u>Filter options</u>**<u>:</u> On the top right corner of the notebook view, click on the filter icon.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-5.16.33-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="" />

2. **<u>Apply filters</u>**<u>:</u> Once you click the filter icon, a dropdown menu with available filter options will appear.

   - **Subscribers** – This filter allows you to see notes based on who is subscribed to them.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-5.18.32-PM.png" width="1440" height="752" alt="" />

     - Select the subscriber(s) you want to filter from the list.

     - Click on “**Apply**“.

   - **Sort By** – Click on the “**Sort by**” option. You will see three sorting options:\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-5.20.49-PM.png" width="1440" height="753" alt="" />

     - **Date**: Sort notes by the date they were created or last modified.

     - **Name**: Sort notes alphabetically by their title.

     - **Color**: Sort notes based on their color labels.

3. The notes will automatically rearrange based on your selection.
