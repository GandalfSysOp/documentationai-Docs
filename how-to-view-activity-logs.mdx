---
title: How to view activity logs
---
The activity logs in ProofHub is a comprehensive record of all updates made to your account data, including who performed each activity.

## **View logs**

1. **<u>Manage section:</u>** Select the **“View logs”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-4.47.55-PM.png" width="593" height="752" alt="" />

2. Activity logs are divided into four categories:

   - **Account**

   - **People**

   - **Projects**

   - **Templates**

## **Filter logs**

1. Click on the filter icon in the top-right corner of the activity logs page.

2. **<u>Select filters:</u>** Depending on the category you have selected, different filters will be available.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-20-at-4.50.24-PM.png" width="1440" height="751" alt="" />

   \
   For the **Projects** category, you can use the following filters:

   - **Status:** Filter activities by project status (e.g., Active).

   - **Project:** Select specific projects to filter the activities.

   - **Section:** Filter activities by different sections within the projects.

   - **Users:** Filter activities by specific users.

   - **Date:** Filter activities within a specific date range.

3. **<u>Apply filters:</u>** Select the desired filters and click “**Apply”** to narrow down the activity logs based on your selected criteria.
