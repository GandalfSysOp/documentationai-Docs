## **My preferences**

1. **<u>Accessing notification settings:</u>** Click on your profile icon located at the bottom left corner of the ProofHub interface and select “**Notifications**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-07-01-at-3.40.01-PM.png" width="458" height="570" alt="Personal Settings in ProofHub" />

2. **<u>My preferences:</u>** Under “**My preferences**” you will find two main sections to customize your notifications:\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-10.47.40-AM.png" width="743" height="407" alt="" />

   - **<u>Activity notifications:</u>**

     - **Desktop sound and notifications**: Check or uncheck this option to enable or disable sound alerts and desktop notifications for activity updates.

   - **<u>Chat notifications:</u>**

     - **Desktop sound and notifications**: Check or uncheck this option to enable or disable sound alerts and desktop notifications for chat messages.

3. Click the “**Update**” button to save your notification settings.

## **Language, timezone, and date format**

1. **<u>Accessing language and timezone preferences</u>**<u>:</u> Select “**Language, timezone and date**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-07-01-at-3.41.51-PM.png" width="410" height="571" alt="Language, Timezone and Date in ProofHub" />

2. In the same settings menu, you can also adjust your language and timezone preferences to ensure that ProofHub aligns with your local time and preferred language.

   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-10.57.51-AM.png" width="747" height="484" alt="" />

   - **Select your timezone**:

     - From the dropdown menu, choose the timezone that matches your current location.

   - **Select language**:

     - Choose your preferred language from the available options.

   - **Select date format**:

     - Choose the date format that you prefer (e.g., MM/DD/YYYY, DD/MM/YYYY).

   - **Select time format**:

     - Choose the time format you prefer (e.g., 12-hour or 24-hour).

3. Click the “**Update**” button to save your language, timezone, date format, and time format settings.

## **Email preferences**

1. **<u>Accessing email preferences</u>**: Select “**Email preferences**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-07-01-at-3.43.43-PM.png" width="396" height="570" alt="Email Preferences in ProofHub" />

2. ProofHub allows you to customize your email preferences to ensure you receive the right amount of information without being overwhelmed.

   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-11.11.04-AM.png" width="746" height="715" alt="" />

   - **<u>Project progress reports:</u>** Choose the frequency that best suits your needs for staying updated on project progress.

     - **Daily project progress report**: Receive a daily summary of your project’s progress.

     - **Weekly project progress report**: Receive a weekly summary of your project’s progress.

     - **Monthly project progress report**: Receive a monthly summary of your project’s progress.

   - **<u>My activities:</u>** These settings help you stay on top of your own activities and important updates from ProofHub.

     - **Email me a copy of my activities**: Receive an email copy of the activities you perform in ProofHub.

     - **Email me daily agenda at 07:00 AM**: Receive an email with your daily agenda every morning at 7:00 AM.

     - **Email me product related updates**: Stay informed about new features, updates, and product announcements.

   - **<u>Account activities:</u>**

     - **Never send me email notifications**: Opt out of all email notifications.

     - **Send me email digest after every one hour**: Receive an email digest of your notifications every hour.

     - **Send me email digest after every four hours**: Receive an email digest of your notifications every four hours.

     - **Immediately send me email notifications**: Receive email notifications as soon as activities occur.

3. Click the “**Update**” button to save your email settings.

## **Profile**

1. **<u>Accessing profile settings:</u>** Select “**Profile**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-07-01-at-3.45.30-PM.png" width="400" height="571" alt="Profile in ProofHub" />

2. The “**Profile**” tab in ProofHub allows you to manage your personal information and ensure that your account details are up-to-date.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-11.16.35-AM.png" width="743" height="562" alt="" />

   - **<u>First name:</u>** Enter your first name.

   - **<u>Last name:</u>** Enter your last name.

   - **<u>Email:</u>** Enter your current email address in the email field. Ensure this is a valid and frequently checked email address to receive important notifications and updates.

   - **<u>Title:</u>** Enter your job title or role within the organization. This helps others in your team understand your position and responsibilities.

   - **<u>Profile picture:</u>** Choose a new image from your computer to upload.

3. Click the “**Update**” button to save your changes.
