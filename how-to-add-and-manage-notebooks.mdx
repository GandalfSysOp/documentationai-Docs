title: How to add and manage notebooks
---
## **Add a notebook**

1. **<u>Navigate to the notes section:</u>** Select the specific project where you want to create the notebook. Click the “**Notes**” tab on the left-side menu.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/1-1.png" width="590" height="559" alt="" />

   \
   \


2. **<u>Add a notebook:</u>** Click on the **“+”** icon to initiate the creation of a new notebook.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/2-1.png" width="690" height="547" alt="" />

   \
   \


3. **<u>Enter notebook title:</u>** Enter a title for your notebook.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/3-1.png" width="715" height="213" alt="" />

   \


   **<u>Note:</u>****&#x20;***If you want this notebook to be easily accessible and visible at the top of the notes list, consider using the “****Pin to Top****” option.*

4. **<u>Save the notebook:</u>** After entering the title, click the “**Add**” button to save your new notebook.

---

## **Edit a notebook**

1. Click the three-dots icon or right-click on the notebook and select “**Edit**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-5.25.34-PM.png" width="565" height="753" alt="" />

2. **<u>Edit the details:</u>** Choose this option to modify the notebook title or change the “**Pin to Top**” option. After making changes, click “**Update**” to save.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/4-6.png" width="715" height="217" alt="" />

---

## **Delete a notebook**

1. Click the three-dots icon or right-click on the notebook and select “**Delete**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-5.28.35-PM.png" width="535" height="752" alt="" />

2. **<u>Delete:</u>** Select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to delete.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/2-6.png" width="412" height="210" alt="" />

   \
   **<u>Note</u>**: *This action will also delete all notes within the notebook.*

---

## **Create a copy of the notebook**

1. Click the three-dots icon or right-click on the notebook and select “**Create a copy**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Screenshot-2024-05-24-at-12.56.08-PM.png" width="558" height="753" alt="" />

2. **<u>Create a copy:</u>** This option allows you to duplicate the notebook. A pop-up window will prompt you to rename the notebook title and select the project where the copy will be saved.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/1-9.png" width="715" height="310" alt="" />

---

## **Move notebook**

1. Click the three-dots icon or right-click on the notebook and select “**Move**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-5.31.39-PM.png" width="530" height="752" alt="" />

2. **<u>Move:</u>** Select this option to move the notebook to another project. A pop-up window will appear, prompting you to choose the destination project.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/1-6.png" width="716" height="325" alt="" />

   \
   **<u>Note:</u>** *Moving a notebook will also move and add any subscribed people to the new project.*

---

## **Bookmark notebook**

1. Click the three-dots icon or right-click on the notebook and select “**Bookmark**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Screenshot-2024-05-22-at-5.33.13-PM.png" width="527" height="749" alt="" />

2. The bookmarked notebook will appear in the right-side menu with other saved bookmarks.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/04/Screenshot-2024-05-24-at-9.25.33-AM.png" width="427" height="394" alt="" />

---
