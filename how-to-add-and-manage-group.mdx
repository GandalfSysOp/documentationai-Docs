title: How to add and manage group
---
## **Add group**

1. **<u>Navigate to the people section</u>****:** In the top-left corner of the **“People”** section, find and click the **“+”** button to initiate adding a group.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/People-group-1-2.png" width="526" height="394" alt="" />

2. **<u>Provide group details</u>****:** A new window will open for creating the group.

   - **<u>Group name: </u>**&#x45;nter a clear and descriptive name for your group.

   - **<u>Add members</u>****:** Select the individual users you want to include in this group.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/People-group-2.png" width="699" height="285" alt="" />

3. **<u>Finalize group creation</u>****:** Once you’ve added the desired members, click the “**Add”** button to finalize creating the group.

---

## **Edit Group**

1. **<u>Navigate to the people section</u>****:** Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the group and select **“Edit”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Group-edit.png" width="568" height="334" alt="" />

2. **<u>Save changes</u>****:** Modify the group name, add or remove a user from your account. After making the changes, click the **“Update”** button to save.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/group-edit-2.png" width="698" height="284" alt="" />

---

## **Delete group**

1. **<u>Navigate to the people section</u>****:** Click on the three dots icon or right-click on the group and select **“Delete”**.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Group-delete.png" width="568" height="334" alt="" />

2. **<u>Delete group</u>****:** Click **“Yes”** on the confirmation window to delete the group from your ProofHub account.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/delete-group-1.png" width="489" height="244" alt="" />

   \
   **<u>Note</u>**: *While deleting a group, you will have the option to move existing people to another group*.\
   *ProofHub keeps items in the “Trash” for 15 days. Restore them easily by going to Trash. For detailed instructions on restoring deleted items from the trash, please refer to&#xA0;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/manage/how-to-manage-trash" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*Trash*</a>*.*

---

## **Search group**

1. In order to find any particular group from your account, click on the **“Search icon”** at the top-left of the section.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/search-group-1.png" width="548" height="406" alt="" />

2. Now, enter the name of the group and it will display the results for your search.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/search-group-2.png" width="540" height="402" alt="" />
