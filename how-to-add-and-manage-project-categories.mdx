---
title: How to add and manage project categories
---
Project categories in ProofHub help you organize and group projects effectively, making it easier to manage and locate related projects. Follow these steps to manage your project categories:

## **Add a category**

1. **<u>Manage section</u>**<u>:</u> Select the “**Manage”** section and select “**Project categories**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.48.17-PM.png" width="590" height="754" alt="" />

2. **<u>Add a new category:</u>**\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.51.19-PM.png" width="1440" height="754" alt="" />

   - Click the **“Add”** button.

   - Enter a title and click **“Add”**.\


     <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.52.39-PM.png" width="714" height="188" alt="" />

---

## **Edit a category**

1. Right-click or click on the three dots and select “**Edit**”.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.56.56-PM.png" width="1039" height="203" alt="" />

2. From the edit window make changes as required and click “**Update**“.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-12.58.04-PM.png" width="715" height="185" alt="" />

---

## **Delete a category**

1. Either right-click or click on the three dots and select **“Delete**”.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.01.43-PM.png" width="1042" height="207" alt="" />

2. Select “**Yes**” within this confirmation window to proceed with the deletion.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-17-at-1.02.40-PM.png" width="443" height="176" alt="" />

**<u>Note:</u>** *ProofHub keeps items in the “Trash” for 15 days. Restore them easily by going to Trash. For detailed instructions on restoring deleted items from the trash, please refer to&#xA0;*<a href="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/manage/how-to-manage-trash" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">*Trash*</a>*.*
