The “**Me view**” is your landing page whenever you log in to ProofHub. It serves as your central hub, providing a consolidated view of everything on your plate, and helping you stay focused and organized.

## **Jump to**

1. Click on the “**Jump to**” bar located within the ProofHub interface. Alternatively, use the keyboard shortcut **Ctrl** or **Command + J** to access the “**Jump to**” section.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-23-at-10.48.52-AM.png" width="1063" height="275" alt="" />

2. **<u>Search for an item</u>**<u>:</u> In the “**Jump to**” section, start typing the name of the project, bookmark, or person you want to navigate to.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-23-at-10.47.59-AM.png" width="1440" height="753" alt="" />

3. As you type, the search function will filter and display relevant options.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-1.37.47-PM.png" width="986" height="508" alt="" />

4. **<u>Select from recent projects, assigned projects, people, or bookmarks</u>**<u>:</u>\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/05/Screenshot-2024-05-21-at-1.36.40-PM.png" width="978" height="499" alt="" />

   - In the “**Jump to**” section, you’ll find a list of options, including:

     - Recent projects: View projects you’ve recently accessed.

     - All assigned projects: See a list of all projects you’re assigned to.

     - Users for chat: Access a list of team members with whom you can chat.

     - Your bookmarks: View your bookmarked items for quick access.

5. **<u>Note:</u>**

   - *Clicking on a project name in the “****Jump to****” section will redirect you to the project overview tab, providing a quick overview of the project’s status and progress.*

   - *Clicking on a person’s name will open their chat window, enabling seamless communication and collaboration with team members.*
