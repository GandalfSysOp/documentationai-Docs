---
title: Can't Receive Notifications
description: Quick fix for missing notifications in ProofHub.
---

## Issue

- No email or in-app notifications
- Team missing important updates

## Possible Causes

- Notifications turned off in settings
- Email filters or spam

## Resolution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Notification Settings">
    Go to your avatar > "My Preferences" > "Notifications". Ensure notifications are enabled for your activity.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Email Spam/Junk Folder">
    Check your spam. Mark ProofHub emails as 'Not Spam'.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Logging Out and In Again">
    Sometimes refreshing your session restores notifications.
  </Step>
</Steps>
