## Adding Users

You can easily add team members to your ProofHub account.

1. Go to the "People" tab in the top menu.
2. Click "Add" and select "User".
3. Enter the new member's email address.
4. Set their role and permissions.
5. Click "Invite".

The user will receive an email invitation to join your ProofHub workspace.

If you need to add multiple users, repeat these steps or use bulk add from the same menu.
