## Steps to Create a Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Projects Section">
    Click on the "Projects" tab in the main navigation bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add New Project">
    Click the "+ New Project" button.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Details">
    Enter the project name, description, and choose a color.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Team Members">
    Select people to add and assign roles (admin, member, client).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create">
    Click "Create Project" to finish. Your new project will now appear in your workspace.
  </Step>
</Steps>
