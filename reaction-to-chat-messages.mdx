---
title: Reaction to chat messages
---
Reactions on chat messages allow you to quickly and easily express your thoughts without typing a full response. Whether it’s a thumbs-up for agreement or a smiley face for something funny, adding reactions to messages enhances communication within your team.

**Why use reactions?**

1. **<u>Quick feedback:</u>**<u> </u>Reactions provide a fast way to acknowledge or respond to messages without needing to type.

2. **<u>Enhanced engagement:</u>** Keep conversations lively and engaging by reacting to team members’ messages.

3. **<u>Clarity:</u>** Use reactions to communicate your response clearly, even in busy chat threads.

---

## **React to a message.**

1. **<u>Hover over the message:</u>**<u> </u>Move your cursor over the message you would like to react to. An emoji icon will appear next to the message. Click on the emoji icon that appears.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Reaction-hover-over-the-message.png" width="302" height="478" alt="Reaction - hover over the message in ProofHub" />

2. **<u>Choose your reaction:</u>**<u> </u>Browse through the available emojis and click on the one that best represents your reaction. Your chosen emoji will then appear beneath the message.\


   <Image src="https://help.proofhub.com/swift/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/Reaction-message-2-1.png" width="300" height="477" alt="Reaction message 2 in ProofHub" />



---

## **Managing your reactions**

1. **<u>Change your reaction:</u>**<u> </u>If you change your mind, simply click on the emoji icon again and select a different reaction. Your new reaction will replace the old one.

2. **<u>Remove a reaction:</u>** Hover over the message, click on the emoji icon, and then click on the selected emoji to remove it.
