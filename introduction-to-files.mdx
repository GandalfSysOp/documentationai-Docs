title: Introduction to files
---
Files in ProofHub offer a seamless and efficient way to manage project-related documents, facilitating collaboration and streamlining workflows. Here’s why the file management feature in ProofHub is truly remarkable:

1. **<u>Centralized storage</u>**: Users can store all project-related files, including PDFs, images, spreadsheets, and more, in one centralized location. This eliminates the need to search through multiple platforms or folders, saving valuable time and ensuring all team members have access to the latest versions of files.

2. **<u>Easy sharing and collaboration</u>**: ProofHub simplifies the process of sharing files with team members and stakeholders. Users can upload files directly from their computer or integrate with popular third-party applications such as Dropbox, Box, and OneDrive. This flexibility allows for seamless collaboration, regardless of where files are originally stored.

3. **<u>Effortless proofing</u>**: The built-in proofing tools empower teams to review and provide feedback on files directly within the platform. Whether it’s reviewing a design mockup, providing edits on a document, or annotating an image, ProofHub’s proofing capabilities streamline the feedback process and ensure clear communication among team members.

4. **<u>Version control</u>**: Version control features enable users to track changes and manage different iterations of files effectively. This ensures that everyone is working on the most up-to-date version of a file.
